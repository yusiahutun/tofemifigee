Paris Hilton and husband Carter Reum celebrate Easter skiing at members-only Yellowstone Club in MT

October 2024 · 4 minute read

New parents Paris Hilton and Carter Reum celebrated Easter by skiing at the world's only private members-only resort The Yellowstone Club in Big Sky, MT.

The married couple of 17 months are among the elite 864 members - which reportedly include Gisele Bündchen, Ben Affleck, Jennifer Lopez, Justin Timberlake, Jessica Biel, Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerberg, and Warren Buffett.

Only homeowners can become Yellowstone Club members and there's a long waiting list for condos costing around $6M-$30M on top of the initial $400K fee and $60K/year dues.

The 42-year-old hotel heiress-turned-reality TV icon enjoyed 'sliving down the slopes' of the Rocky Mountain resort, which boasts '29K powdery acres' with 18 ski lifts and 100 largely vacant runs.

In 2021, a group of Jamaican cooks, bartenders, and housekeepers settled a discrimination lawsuit against the club: 'They watched other workers at the club - who were not black or Jamaican - do the same work as them, but be treated entirely differently.'

'Happy Easter!' New parents Paris Hilton (R) and Carter Reum (L) celebrated Easter by skiing at the world's only private members-only resort The Yellowstone Club in Big Sky, MT

Paris - who boasts 60.6M social media followers - wore a pink $34 'Rainbow Heart Flip Ringer T-shirt' from children clothing company Lola + The Boys beneath a black hoodie and coral ski jacket.

Hilton finished off her sporty attire with white Bogner-brand star-spangled ski pants and a pink bunny ears hat in honor of the Christian holiday.

When she was finished skiing, the Paris: The Memoir author slipped her size 11 feet into a $305 pair of Moon Boots in 'Icon Metallic-Gold.'

Missing from Paris' slideshow was three-month-old infant son Phoenix Barron Hilton Reum, whom she welcomed via surrogate in January.

Hilton and the 42-year-old M13 co-founder already went through the IVF/egg-harvesting process during the pandemic and their fraternal twins (daughter London and son named after mystery city) will be the next to arrive.

Meanwhile, VH1 Secrets of Aspen alum Laura Bellizzi told Page Six in 2021 that Carter has only seen their 10-year-old daughter 'once since she was born.'

But it wasn't all about fun and skiing as the celebutante DJ arrived in Montana last Thursday to help lobby House Bill 218, which would ratchet up restrictions on troubled teen boarding schools, with Rep. Laura Smith.

Paris - who was allegedly assaulted, strangled, malnourished, and ogled at naked by staff at the since-closed Provo Canyon School in Utah at age 17 - handed 50 individual signed letters for each state Senator.
