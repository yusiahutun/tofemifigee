Josh Gates' Love Story With Hallie Gnatovich

Back in 2007, Josh Gates was starring as the host of a Syfy channel's show named Destination Truth.

There he went on to meet the woman who was going to bring unprecedented joy in his life, a woman he was going to take wedding vows with, a co-star of the show at the time named Hallie Gnatovich.

After getting introduced, the duo soon began to carry a romantic connection. They worked together for 5 years from 2007 to 2012. During the time, their love affair had well and truly taken off. The couple later got engaged in May 2013.

After multiple dates that went on for years and a year after their cute engagement, the couple tied the knot on 13th September 2014 in a Hidden Pond Resort in Kennebunkport, Maine.
