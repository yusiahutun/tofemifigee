This is the sickening moment three brutes shot and robbed an innocent man after he gave them a dollar at a gas station in Kansas City, Missouri.

Police are hunting for the three suspects who are accused of shooting the man on February 22 around 10.45pm in the 4500 block of Cleveland Avenue. 

The shocking incident was captured on CCTV and shows the victim handing over a dollar to one of the suspects he thought was a homeless man, before two other men emerge from the gas station, shoot him, go through his pockets and steal his car. 

The victim is now out of hospital and recovering at home. 

Police are searching for the three men, who can be seen on CCTV from inside the store wearing dark tracksuits with their hoods up.

Police shared photos of three suspects who have not been identified as of Monday afternoon

In the shocking video, the victim can be seen walking around his car to hand a dollar to one of the suspects, who he believed to be a homeless person. 

Two other suspects then run out of the gas station and approach the victim, who backs away. 

One of the suspects then shoots him in the leg and he falls to the ground by his car. 

The suspects then begin to climb into the victim's car to drive away when one of them decides to go back and search his pockets.
