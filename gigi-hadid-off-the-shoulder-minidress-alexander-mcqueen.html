Gigi Hadid's Off-the-Shoulder Minidress Includes a Graphic Eyeball Print

February 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Leave it to Gigi Hadid to bring the glamour.

Yesterday, the model had a whirlwind day in New York City, where she stepped out in six different outfits to promote the second season of Netflix's fashion design competition show, Next in Fashion.

She capped off the day in a jaw-dropping ivory gown from Alexander McQueen's spring/summer 2023 collection. The draped, asymmetrical minidress featured puffy off-the-shoulder sleeves and a dramatic red-and-black print of an eyeball. She accented the look with silver jewelry, also from Alexander McQueen; sheer gray tights; and red-hot slingback pumps from Christian Louboutin.

Earlier in the day, Hadid appeared on The Tonight Show wearing a head-to-toe forest green look courtesy of Alexandre Vauthier's spring/summer 2023 couture collection. The outfit included a hooded leather jacket, matching leggings, and heeled boots.

Getty Images

During a Good Morning America appearance, the model pulled a look from Valentino's pre-fall 2023 collection consisting of a gray ribbed knit crop top, a gray midi skirt with a hip-high leg slit adorned with bows, gray knit opera gloves, and bright red pumps. She layered up with gray sheer tights from Calzedonia.

Jose Perez/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

And for her third talk show appearance, Hadid visited the TODAY show looking sleek in a beige short suit by Brandon Maxwell, which she finished with sparkling pumps.

Getty Images

While posing with Next in Fashion host Tan France in Times Square, Hadid embodied biker chic in an all-black look composed of a Mugler catsuit, a leather bomber jacket from The Frankie Shop, and slouchy leather boots. She accessorized with black sunglasses and a diamond choker.

Getty Images

Finally, the model looked cool and casual in her sixth outfit, which featured a white T-shirt that cinched at her waist, wide-leg jeans with a white graphic print, and white heeled booties.
