Queen Latifah was spotted with her longtime partner, choreographer Eboni Nichols in West Hollywood on Saturday night. 

The parents of Rebel, four, exited the celeb hotspot The Bird Streets Club on the Sunset Strip in West Hollywood.

The Last Holiday star, 53, wore a black V-neck shirt and wide-legged black pants. 

She covered up in a camouflage jacket featuring patches down the arms and accessorized with large silver earrings. 

Eboni, 43, wore a long-sleeved black dress that fell to just above her knee and knee-high black boots.

Latifah, who was born Dana Owens, and Nichols have been dating since 2013.

Eboni is a professional dancer and choreographer who has toured with some of the biggest names in music including Beyoncé, Rihanna and Usher.

She graduated from Chapman University in Southern California with a degree in Theatre and Dance.

Eboni also spent some time dancing as a Laker Girl for the Los Angeles Lakers basketball time.

Latifah and Eboni met in 2009 while the Just Wright star was on Dancing With the Stars. 

The couple are notoriously private. So much so that the All Hail the Queen rapper didn't even mention Eboni publicly until eight years into their relationship. 

Latifah publicly acknowledged her partner Eboni and son Rebel for the first time during her acceptance speech for the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 2021 BET Awards.

She closed her speech with a 'Happy Pride' message before saying, 'Eboni, my love; Rebel, my love' while placing her hand on her heart.
