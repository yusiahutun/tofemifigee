Vanessa Hudgens' red carpet lace bra look is a moment

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Sarah Hyland called the former Disney star "iconic"

    Vanessa Hudgens's style evolution has been fascinating to watch since her days – fans never saw a sheer gothic queen look coming.

    The 33-year-old former star paired a pretty black lace bra with a sweeping black maxi skirt to walk the red carpet at Monday evening's CFDA Fashion Awards in New York. Vanessa's choice of minimal attire showcased her figure and she looked rather graceful with her long ivory robe trailing behind her. Vanessa wore custom Vera Wang and it was her striking hair transformation that completed the look…
