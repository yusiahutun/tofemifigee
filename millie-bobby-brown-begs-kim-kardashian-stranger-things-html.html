Millie Bobby Brown begs for Kim Kardashian to be on Stranger Things

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Millie Bobby Brown has launched an ambitious bid to get Kim Kardashian as her new co-star on Stranger Things.  

The 14-year-old was asked by E! News which person she wishes would make a cameo on the Netflix series, and in her response admitted that she loves the Kardashians.

Millie, who was asked to pick a potential castmate while on the red carpet of a Netflix event earlier this month, gasped 'Yes, a Kardashian!' with beaming eyes—even though Kim is not an actor.

Biggest fan: Millie Bobby Brown (pictured with Kim Kardashian in March) has named the reality star as the person she wishes would make an appearance on Stranger Things

BFFs: The 14-year-old has said in the past she is a huge fan of the Kardashians, and hung out with Kim earlier this year in Los Angeles

She's got it all planned out: Millie, who plays a character named Eleven on the Netflix series, said Kim could play a person named Ten 

Millie then turned to her Stranger Things co-star Noah Schnapp, 13, asking him to weigh in. 

'Noah, would you love to see Kim Kardashian in Stranger Things?' Millie asked Noah while he was posing for the cameras. 

He immediately stopped what he was doing and said, 'Wait, she's going to be in Stranger Things?''

Noah seemed flustered and confused as Millie continued joking, adding: 'Yeah, she is. She's going to play Ten.'

Millie's character on the show is named Eleven, hence her pick for Kim's possible role.

The young actress then turned back to the camera, adding: 'Yep. Kim Kardashian's going to be on Stranger Things, you guys. It's going to be amazing.'

When asked about her love for Kim she admitted, 'Yes I am a huge fan of the Kardashians.'

At this point, Noah popped back in front of the camera and said, 'If you're watching, hi Kim!'

Kim herself seems rather on board with Millie's idea. 

After hearing about Millie wanting Kim on the show, the 37-year-old tweeted out a link to the E! News segment, writing, '10!!!!!,' with a hands-up emoji.

This isn't the first time Millie has opened up about her affection for the Kardashian clan.

Back in November 2017, she confessed her love for the family during an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.

'I'm obsessed with them,' Millie said at the time. 'They're so entertaining — they have their own language.'

Things escalated when Millie and Kim finally met.

They took to Instagram to share snaps of their encounter, which occurred in LA at a mysterious location.

The two seemed thick as thieves as they posed for selfies together, playing around with video filters.
