Kate Upton poses with Atlanta Braves brothers as swimsuit model ditches her bikini for baseball uniform!

Famous for her appearances in two Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issues, Kate Upton has ditched her bikini for a baseball uniform to pose for the magazine’s Major League Baseball Playoffs review this week.

Pictured with brothers Justin Upton and B.J Upton, who star for the Atlanta Braves, the 21-year-old has become the first swimsuit model ever to grace the front cover of the weekly edition of the popular magazine.

Covering up for these snaps, her previous showings saw her in just a pair of white bikini bottoms and a parka jacket for this year's swimsuit edition while in 2012 she posed in just a red bikini.

Cover star: Kate Upton has become the first swimsuit model to appear in the magazine's weekly edition

Namesakes: The three Uptons posed together for the magazine's Major League Baseball Playoffs review

Uncovered: Kate's new Sports Illustrated cover is a far cry from her past appearances in the magazine

With the Braves in town to take on the Mets, the New Yorkers’ home of Citi Field provided the backdrop for last month's shoot as the beauty, who joked the Upton brothers were her long lost cousins, posed with her baseball-playing namesakes.

Despite no real allegiance to any particular baseball team, Upton has previously dated Detroit Tigers player Justin Verlander.

Meanwhile, the Upton brothers face the Los Angeles Dodgers on Thursday in the Braves’ first postseason game.

Strike a pose: The shoot took place last month at the home of the New York Mets baseball team, Citi Field.
