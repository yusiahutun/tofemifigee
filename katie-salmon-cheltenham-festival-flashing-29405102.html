Love Island stars were banned from racing after flashing boobs at Cheltenham Festival

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Two former Love Island stars were banned from Cheltenham and other UK racecourses after flashing their boobs while attending the iconic Festival.

OnlyFans star and lingerie model Katie Salmon attended the 2016 festival with her fellow Love Islander Jessica Hayes when the stunning models flashed their boobs at photographers from a hospitality box. Then aged 20, Salmon came under criticism for her drunken antics when the pair flaunted their assets in a set of plunging dresses.

Afterwards she wrote on Facebook: “I love how people are making out a nipple is the end of the world. We were drunk ... being young girls having fun. I apologise if I offended anybody, but really, have you guys not seen a nipple before?”

She previously told the Daily Star: "I have portrayed the wrong image in the past and it’s not something I still want to be known for. It comes up a lot and still to this day I am always referred to as the Cheltenham girl."

Salmon is not afraid to showcase her body on her Instagram page, which has over 269,000 followers regularly posting pictures in hardly there lingerie outfits. On the adult-only OnlyFans page, 'The Scouse Goddess' has accumulated over 1,700 likes and says "My content, charm and intelligence will get you hooked."

Despite being confident to showcase her body, Salmon said she would not repeat her Cheltenham antics.

People in this day and age judge you by how you behave and even when there is drink involved or whatever, obviously everyone behaves differently around that, but people are too uptight these days.

“Being in the spotlight now I have to be wary of the example I am setting.”
