The game is an imaginary play that brings into context planetary engineering seeking to extend human habitation to mars by deliberately changing its climate. 7 Wonders is one of the most famous board games like Betrayal that are mission-oriented. The game is a complete depiction of military conflicts, ancient civilization, and commercial activity. The fact that the game has won an excess of 30 gaming awards makes it suitable for most gamers out there.

4. Pandemic board game

Having played House on a Hill game successfully, it is about time you spiced up things with Pandemic. This co-operative board game is based primarily on the idea of four pandemic outbreaks that threats regional extinctions.

The game comes with a two or four-player set. Unlike Mansion on the Hill board game which is based on competition, Pandemic is based on co-operation. It is daunting to discover the four cures before it is too late.
