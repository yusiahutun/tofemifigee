Kendall Jenner Accents Her Quietly Cool Suit With an Orange Bag

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Kendall Jenner pushed the envelope for modern suiting with her latest street-style sighting.

The supermodel was recently photographed in Beverly Hills wearing an elegant navy blue suit. Though today’s trends often demand suits appear boxy or oversize, Jenner put her own sophisticated flair on the look, wearing a Bottega Veneta set with a wrap-cinched waist, with square pocket flaps seemingly protruding from the center. The pants featured a pleated detail and a straight-leg silhouette. She capped off the outfit with shiny black pumps, narrow black rectangular sunglasses, and gold earrings.

Her most notable accessory, however, proved her color theory skills. Jenner upped the ante with the unexpected piece, complementing the dark blue of her suit with Bottega Veneta's Kalimero Città bag in a vivid orange.

BACKGRID

Jenner’s suit moment is the latest in a streak of fashionable and refined looks she has recently shown off.

Earlier this month, the model jetted to Paris, where she headed out for a glam evening in a crimson skirt suit that featured a corseted bodice, a pleated peplum detail, and a fitted pencil skirt. She accessorized her look with black sunglasses, silver earrings, a black leather purse, and a bold red lip.

And in February, she brought her cool street style to Miami, where she was photographed on a night out in a stretchy dark blue set. The two-piece consisted of a long-sleeved top with a bateau neckline and a drop waist, as well as pants with a flared trumpet silhouette and a split hem at the back.
