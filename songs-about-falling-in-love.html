33 Best Songs about Falling in Love

June 2024 · 3 minute read
1

"I Knew I Loved You" by Savage Garden

Just try not to swoon over these lyrics from this 1999 hit: "I know that it might sound / More than a little crazy but I believe / I knew I loved you before I met you / I think I dreamed you into life."

2

"When You Say Nothing At All" by Alison Krauss

This super-romantic song was made popular by Alison Krauss. We also love the Ronan Keating version from the Notting Hill soundtrack.

3

"I Love You Always Forever" by Donna Lewis

This 1996 hit by Donna Lewis is a certified bop.

4

"Shallow" by Lady Gaga, Bradley Cooper

The Oscar-winning song from the 2018 A Star is Born soundtrack never fails to give us chills, especially when Gaga lets go with that incredible voice.

5

"Just The Way You Are" by Bruno Mars

An upbeat rundown of everything Bruno Mars loves about his lady, this song perfectly captures that moment in love when everything is, well, perfect.

6

"At Last" by Etta James

Almost achingly romantic, this classic from 1960 is all about finally, finally finding love.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

"Truly Madly Deeply" by Savage Garden

When you fall in love you promise the world to "the one"—and mean every word.

8

"This Kiss" by Faith Hill

Sure, this 1998 mega-hit from Faith Hill is super sweet, but then so is falling in love.

9

"Falling Slowly" by Glen Hansard, Marketa Irglova

Another Oscar-winner, this hauntingly delicate song from the film Once reminds us that falling in love doesn't always have to happen hard and fast.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

"We Found Love" by Rihanna feat. Calvin Harris

This 2011 electro house smash is best enjoyed with your love on the dance floor.

11

"Butterflies" by Kacey Musgraves

What's better than that tickle in your tummy the right one gives you?

12

"All I Want Is You" by U2

This slow-burner from the Irish band's "Rattle and Hum" album launched a thousand weddings in the late 80s.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

"Unapologize" by Carrie Underwood

There's nothing harder than being the first one to say "I love you."

14

"Can't Help Falling In Love" by Elvis

One of the greatest love songs of all time, "Can't Help Falling in Love" was first released on Elvis's 1961 "Blue Hawaii" album, though everyone from Bob Dylan to UB40 has covered it.

15

"Conversations in the Dark" by John Legend

This cut from John Legends's 2020 album, Bigger Love, is a gorgeous, heartfelt song you can just picture him singing to wife Chrissy.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

"Love Story" by Taylor Swift

Written about a guy Taylor Swift almost dated, "Love Story" follows the plotline of the play Romeo and Juliet—until the song's happy ending.

17

"Walking On Sunshine" by Katrina and the Waves

This feel-good favorite from the 80s is just like discovering your sweetie loves you just as much as you love him.

18

"Beautiful Crazy" by Luke Combs

Some loves are a little crazy and that's a beautiful thing, according to this lovely, old school-sounding country tune.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

19

"Head Over Feet" by Alanis Morissette

Let's be honest. After Alanis Morissette's ticked-off anthem "You Oughta Know," it was kind of a relief to hear this adorable ode to falling in love from her.

20

"Crazy in Love" Beyoncé ft. JAY-Z

Winner of a Grammy Award for Best R&B Song, this collaboration between Queen Bey and her knight JAY-Z is almost insanely catchy.
