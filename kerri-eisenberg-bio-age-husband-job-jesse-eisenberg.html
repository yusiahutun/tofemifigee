Fleishman Is in Trouble star, Jesse Eisenberg isn’t the only actor in his family. His sister, Kerri Eisenberg has also graced several TV shows. Infact, she ran a performing arts school and creative therapy practice in Chelsea, NYC for 10 years!

Meet Kerri Eisenberg, Jesse Eisenberg Older Sister

Kerri Eisenberg is Jesse Eisenberg’s older sister. However, the two are half-siblings.

She was born to parents, Barry E and Amy C Eisenberg (born: Mar 1954). Reportedly, her dad and mom got married in 1977 when they were both 23. Sadly, they divorced soon after and Kerri’s dad went on to marry Jesse’s mother Amy Fishman (who is very close to Kerri).

Jesse then arrived in this world on October 5, 1983. And then arrived Kerri’s little sister Hallie Eisenberg (born: Aug 2, 1992). This means Hallie turned 27 in 2019. On her birthday then, Kerri featured her on his IG post captioned, “’ I’m so proud of you and all that I hear you’re doing … you always have a place to stay in Harlem, you know.”

Likewise, Kerri once boasted about her brother Jesse on her IG. She wrote, “My #oneandonly #starfucker moment… my brother #jesseeisenberg.”

Now, talking in detail about Kerri’s biological mother, Amy is very supportive of her daughter. She was even there on the set of Scene Stealer to support Kerri and take care of her dog while she was acting. “Mom- thank you for being there for Krishna and me… it was so so so great to see you and we miss you so much already and all the time,” Kerri thanked her mother later on IG.

Also, In July 2016, she dedicated an IG post to her. It read, “She saves my life every day, makes me laugh, taught me what it’s like to feel loved, and understood things about me that no one else on this planet ever could.”

Did you know: Kerri’s sister Hallie is the Pepsi Girl from the late ’90s and early ’00s commercials. She has also acted in various films as a kid like Paulie (1988), and Beautiful (2000).

Kerri Eisenberg Job

Don’t ask Kerri Eisenberg about her job because the list goes on and on. She claimed she’s a “Public Figure / Drag Artist / Actor / Acting Coach / Artist / Circus / Song/Screen Writer / Director / Model.” And what’s even more remarkable she’s self-taught and self-made.

A compulsive artist, Kerrias has several collections which each include hundreds of pieces. For instance, her “Heart Shaped Heart” drawing includes tattoo designs, self-portraits, and  “Moments” which she calls her short poetry, and there are a ton of them.

Likewise, Heart Shaped Heart — Untitled Clay Sculptures are more than 2000. They were primarily made during the early 2000s when she was going through a difficult period — staying up all night obsessively sculpting and listening to Tori Amos.

Her works have also been in galleries throughout NY and the Tri-State Area including a show at Madison Square Garden. 

Now coming to her time as an actor, she’s acted in theatre, film, and TV shows such as The Politician (2020), Mr. and Mrs. Jaclkson (2019), High Maintenance (2018), Shades of Blue (2016), and more.

As for her education, Kerri attended PPAS NYC Professional Performing Arts School.
