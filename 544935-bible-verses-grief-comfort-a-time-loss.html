20+ Bible verses about grief to comfort you at a time of loss

February 2024 · 6 minute read

In the face of loss, the weight of grief can be overwhelming. The world seems to move on, but the ache in your heart remains. If you're searching for solace and strength during this difficult time, read the Bible – it has several comforting verses. Bible verses about grief are filled with compassion, wisdom and hope.

Grief is a profound and deeply personal journey that people navigate differently. In times of mourning, many find comfort and strength in the enduring words of the Bible. The scripture is rich with verses that speak directly to the heartbroken, offering hope, consolation, and the reassurance of God’s presence even in the darkest moments.
