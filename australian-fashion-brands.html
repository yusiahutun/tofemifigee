15 Australian brands that fashion editors are obsessed with

March 2024 · 6 minute read

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Francois Durand//Getty Images

Matteau

SHOP MATTEAU

Matteau's grown-up take on beachwear has become a real favourite with A-listers over the years, and it's not difficult to see why. With swimwear that is designed to flatter, and dresses and cover-ups that will make you the most elegant woman by the pool, this is a great name to know in Australian fashion.

Sir

SHOP SIR

Sir is a super popular Australian label which is known for its laidback attitude to style, creating pieces that are easy to dress up or down and work for every aspect of a busy woman's life. From ruffled dresses and more toned-down slips to chic tailoring and beautiful swimwear, there are always some seriously irresistible pieces on offer here.

Camilla and Marc

SHOP CAMILLA AND MARC

Contemporary Australian label Camilla and Marc was founded by brother-and-sister duo Camilla Freeman-Topper and Marc Freeman, who have made it their mission to create luxurious easy-to-wear pieces that merge femininity with androgynous shapes. Beloved for both its tailoring and pretty dresses, it's a great label to know for those wardrobe staples.

Aje

SHOP AJE

Another great Aussie label to have on your radar is Aje, a brand that is fast becoming known for its statement, often colourful dresses in bold silhouettes, which you have probably seen (and admired) on your Instagram feed. Created with the aim of bridging the gap between the beach and the city, Aje is all about effortless elegance.

Anna Quan

SHOP ANNA QUAN

If it's sleek shirting, minimal dresses and colourful silk separates you love, then make sure to check out Anna Quan. "It started with the re-imagining of the classic white shirt," she explains. "It continues with clean-lined, considered garments designed to last beyond a single season."

Albus Lumen

SHOP ALBUS LUMEN

Australian stylist Marina Afonina is behind Albus Lumen, a conscious label that is all about giving back to the community; everything is made in Australia, created by local craftspeople using traditional techniques. We love the brand for its exquisitely made separates and dresses, all of which are great for holidays or day-to-day wear.

St. Agni

SHOP ST. AGNI

If you are all about pared-back minimalism and high-quality capsule classics, then let us direct you to St. Agni, a brand which prides itself on timeless design. Chic knits, shirting, simple wrap dresses, cool separates and minimal accessories make up the collections, which are designed to be worn forever.

Christopher Esber

SHOP CHRISTOPHER ESBER

Known for its striking dresses, which often feature bold cut-outs, Christopher Esber has fast become a favourite with the fashion set. The designer hails from Australia and often cites his home as inspiration for his designs, which are all about relaxed fits, menswear-inspired touches and intricate details.

Bird & Knoll

SHOP BIRD & KNOLL

Launching initially with a line of luxury scarves, Bird & Knoll expanded in 2016 into resort wear, which aimed to bring whimsy escapism into everybody's wardrobes. Nodding to the founders' coastal roots, the brand's easy breezy dresses and separates are perfect for summer holidays.

Posse

SHOP POSSE

Posse is one of our favourite summery labels. It boasts relaxed, feminine pieces that are perfect for a modern woman's wardrobe and the brand is proud of its 'less is more' approach – this relates both to the toned-down aesthetic of the pieces, as well as the label's commitment to slow fashion. Posse only produces collections in limited numbers, slowly adding to its core collection when it knows a piece will be guaranteed to sell out, and focuses on high-quality, conscious fabrications with pieces designed in Sydney and brought to life by its artisans in Bali and India.

Michael Lo Sordo

SHOP MICHAEL LO SORDO

Michael Lo Sordo is another big name designer on the Australian fashion scene. He is known best for his beautiful slinky eveningwear and his similarly slinky bridal. First launched in 2006, the brand has now dressed the likes of Margot Robbie, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley, Kim Kardashian and Gigi Hadid.

Bassike

SHOP BASSIKE

Having launched in 2006, Bassike prides itself on creating responsible collections that are created in collaboration with local artisans and suppliers in Australia. All collections are made with a seasonless approach in mind, encouraging its consumers to invest in pieces that they will hold onto forever, including beautiful organic cotton dresses and jumpsuits, high-quality sustainable denim and chic jersey basics.

Zimmermann

SHOP ZIMMERMANN

Zimmermann is probably one of Australia's most famous fashion exports, and it's not hard to see why the wanderlust-inspired designs have always been so popular. From the most beautiful beachy pieces to dresses that will elevate your evening wardrobe, every fashion editor wants to inject her summer with a little dose of Zimmermann.

Alèmais

SHOP ALEMAIS

Another eco-conscious brand hailing from Australia is Alèmais, a label whose mission is to close the gap between style and sustainability by creating pieces that will excite customers enough to wear them forever. Its trademark designs include shirts, dresses and skirts which are made in airy materials (including linen and hemp) and covered in punchy prints that are inspired by the natural world.

Abysse

SHOP ABYSSE

If there is one thing Aussie fashionistas do better than anyone else, it is swimwear. The country boasts some of the most exciting beach designers around, and this includes Abysse, a Byron Bay-based label which is known for its sustainable swimwear and surf gear, all of which is made from recycled plastic bottles and fishing nets.

Oroton

SHOP OROTON

Oroton was founded back in 1938 with a mission of using time-honoured techniques to create beautiful clothing, and working to source some of the best luxury materials in the world. The brand has become known for its premium accessories and modern yet classic clothing collections, most of which are created in neutral, elegant tones.

Rachel Gilbert

SHOP RACHEL GILBERT

If you're looking to add some beautiful occasionwear to your wardrobe, then make sure to check out Rachel Gilbert, an Aussie designer whose statement creations have been winning us over for years. The brand is known for its high-end and often colourful designs, perfect for that next important event or wedding.

Boteh

SHOP BOTEH

Another great Aussie beachwear brand to bookmark for your summer holidays is Boteh. The label is all about easy-breezy bohemian pieces, usually in the most beautiful prints, which have all been created through thoughtful and sustainable production methods.
