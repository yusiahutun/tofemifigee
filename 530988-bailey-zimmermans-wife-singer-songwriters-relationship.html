Who is Bailey Zimmerman?

Bailey is a talented American country and southern rock music singer and songwriter. He was born on 27 January 2000 in Louisville, Illinois, United States. His dad owned a trucking business where he would accompany him to haul cars across state lines.

Even though he has ventured into music, he hails from a family of business people. His father also owns a towing and salvage yard. On the other hand, his grandfather owned a car shop where his mother, Kristi Bailey Haerr, used to work. His brother, Brent L Bailey, is a professional auto and truck collision specialist.

Career

Bailey started playing the guitar when he was only nine. However, he threw it away to play baseball and ride bikes. Later, he started working at a local meat processing plant at 16. In 2018, he relocated to West Virginia, where he started building gas pipelines until his music success in 2021.
