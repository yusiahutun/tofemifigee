What teams has van Persie played for?

Persie started his football career in his home team Feyenoord where he played from 2001 to 2004.

After rubbing shoulders with the club's manager Bert van Marwijk in 2004, he joined English club Arsenal. He joined the club as a long-term replacement for Dennis Bergkamp, a compatriot. He was the club's captain. He played for Arsenal for almost ten years, that is, from 2004 to 2012.

He later joined rival team Manchester United on 17th August 2012 for a four year contact. Robin van Persie had to leave The Red Devils after two consecutive injury-hit seasons.

He then left for Turkish Super League club Fenerbahce in July 2015. His injuries were a significant limitation for him, and he soon went back to his home club Feyenoord on a free transfer where he still is to date.
