Paddock girl Eva Padlock thrills MotoGP fans with latest set of risqu photos

May 2024 · 2 minute read

MotoGP paddock girl Eva Padlock has wowed fans once again after posting her latest Instagram snaps while in coronavirus lockdown.

The Spanish model is building up quite the following on social media and has been sending pulses racing over the last few weeks.

With the 2020 MotoGP season on hold much like pretty much every other sport in the world, Padlock, like many others in currently in lockdown.

But the Monster Girl, who represents the energy drink at races around the world, has been keeping many fans occupied in the meantime.

And the 35-year-old took to Instagram on Thursday to pose for the camera in her latest shoot in what appears to be her bedroom.

In one picture Padlock poses on a bed in black lingerie and in another she wows in a turquoise number.
