Pantone Color Of The Year 2024: Peach Fuzz Outfits That Flatter

August 2024 · 7 minute read

Notice a surge of peach-toned sweaters, dresses, pants, accessories, makeup and more popping up in your favorite stores? That’s because “Peach Fuzz,” the vibrant, spring-like pastel, has recently been named Pantone Color of the Year 2024. It’s a tried-and-true classic color that instantly brightens up any look and easily complements any skin color. Want to try the Peach Fuzz trend but not sure how to incorporate it into your every day wardrobe? We tapped celebrity stylist Eric Himel, who has worked with Giuliana Rancic and Tia Mowry, for his savvy styling tips. Keep reading to see Peach Fuzz-inspired outfits that will leave you gorgeous snd glowing.

What does Peach Fuzz signify?

According to Pantone, Peach Fuzz signifies “closeness and connection” and “warmth and modern elegance.” The color is light, uplifting and warm, and feels like a breath of fresh air. It’s bright and cheery, but still classy and mature, making it the perfect option if you want to add a pop of color to your look. It’s a softer shade, so if bold colors tend to make you nervous, Peach Fuzz is your new best friend. Plus, since peach is associated with youthfulness and vitality, wearing peach-colored clothing or accessories can evoke a sense of vibrancy and playfulness. Better yet, peach is a versatile color that can be both subtle and sophisticated. It can be worn in different shades and tones, making it adaptable to various occasions and personal styles.

What shade of peach is best for your skin tone?

Pantone says that its color of the year 2024, Peach Fuzz, is “softly nestled between pink and orange,” meaning it falls on the warmer side. However, there are many variations of peach, ranging from cool to warm — and Himel explains which shades of peach look best on each skin tone.

“There are cool peach tones with slight gray undertones, and there are warm peach tones with pink and orange undertones, respectively,” says Himel.

“If you have a lighter skin tone with pink or yellow undertones, choose a cool peach tone, which will counteract them. If you have a darker skin tone, choose a peach that has peachy/pink undertones, which pop against the brown hues of your skin. It will give you an instant glow and act as a highlighter.”
