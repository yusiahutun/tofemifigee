Taylor Swift's excitable kiss with Travis Kelce is exposing four green flags

April 2024 · 4 minute read

Taylor Swift’s recent love story is full of surprises and happy coincidences, and her recent kiss with Travis Kelce shows the couple is showing major green flags.

Ever since Taylor Swift and British movie star Joe Alwyn broke up in 2023, fans of the Blank Space crooner have been speculating who will be Taylor’s next epic romance. After six eventful months which include breaking multiple records with her Eras Tour and a short fling with Matty Healy, looks like Tay Tay has found her The 1.

Taylor Swift rushing to kiss Travis breaks the internet

Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift’s body language are tell-tale signs of how romantic their relationship is currently going and everything proves, they are “so in love.”

Travis flew to Argentina to attend one of Taylor’s shows. The internet broke when the music legend later rushed to embrace Travis right after the show ended.

But the steamy kiss was not the only sign fans got from the Mirrorball crooner’s Saturday show. From the couple leaning into each other to Tay Tay never letting go of Travis’ hands, Traylor is now setting relationship goals for fans.

We take a look at their body language to reveal what their non-verbal cues are really saying.

Inside Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s kiss

Continuous embraces

When the singer ran over to embrace Travis, it was clear they were both feeling the same in that moment. It wasn’t just a kiss, the 33-year-old wrapped her arms around his neck. It seems Trav did the same as well, putting his arm around her.

Not only that, but it’s not the only time they’ve been close.

Taylor and Travis were spotted enjoying dinner together on Friday, November 10, and they seemed to maintain their closeness by holding hands all the time.

As per body language theory, a couple who maintain closeness or show the other person their closeness by leaning into them is usually a green flag. It establishes their connection through their body movements.

Enthusiastic Kiss

The way Taylor rushed to hug Travis and lock their lips right after the show ended depicts the passion the celebrity couple share. It’s regarded as a green flag for any relationship to go long-term.

Body language expert Tonya Reiman talked about the importance of enthusiasm in kissing.

She stated: “Kissing is an emotional, biological, and psychological boost,” adding, “If your partner kisses you with less enthusiasm [than before], it’s a red flag.”

The couple also looked into each other’s eyes as they kissed and twirled after the show. This is a key sign over their deep connection.

But, Travis and Taylor’s keep close eye contact on many occasions. Like when they are spotted talking or even taking a walk with each other shows the couple is in a healthy place in their relationship.

“Eye contact is incredibly intimate,” expert Reiman shares. “Eye contact shows trust and emotional openness [and] tells the partner ‘I am yours.’ If your partner once offered up their eyes and suddenly stops, it’s a red flag,” Reiman adds.

Being in sync

As Tay Tay ran over to her athlete, they wrapped around each other in sync. The little twirl they did was also clear they were mirroring each other’s steps and movements. This is another green flag, clearly.

Likewise, they are often spotted always walking in sync, which is a great sign for couples who are looking forward to a long-term commitment.

“Most couples in love mirror each other and thus walk in sync,” Reiman offered. So when a couple is seen walking out of sync or their flow is disrupted, it can show that there’s a potential disconnect between the two.

Sources close to the celebrities also share similar verdicts about the couple.

As one dished out: “Travis and Taylor are a very real couple, they actually spend a lot of one-on-one time together and have developed a very real connection. They have very detailed plans coming up on how to stay together while she’s on tour and he has games.”
