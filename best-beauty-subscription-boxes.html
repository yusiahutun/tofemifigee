15 Best Beauty Subscription Boxes

March 2024 · 8 minute read

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Image courtesy of Neen

Best Hair Care Subscription Box

Prose The Salon Subscription

Best Hair Care Subscription Box

Prose The Salon Subscription

Credit: Prose

One of the standout aspects of certified B Corp Prose’s subscription box is that each of its hair care offerings (and their ingredients) are tailored to your needs. Similar to other boxes on our list, you start the process with a short quiz to narrow down the particulars of each of their formulas—which include shampoo, conditioner, hair masks, and more—and then, your products arrive regularly at your discretion. Plus, you’ll receive 15 percent off on your order just for subscribing.

Delivery frequency: Customizable

Cost: Free to subscribe. Customers also are gifted one free product for every 10 ordered.

Best Subscription Box for Bestsellers

Dermstore BeautyFix Subscription

Best Subscription Box for Bestsellers

Dermstore BeautyFix Subscription

Credit: Dermstore

Dermstore’s BeautyFix subscription features a monthly combination of some of the top skincare, hair care, and makeup brands the retailer carries—and it’s good. Each box contains both full- and travel-size products from coveted brands like Sol de Janeiro, Briogeo, and Wander Beauty.

Delivery frequency: Monthly

Cost: $25 per month

Best Value Subscription Box

Ipsy Glam Bag Subscription Box

Best Value Subscription Box

Ipsy Glam Bag Subscription Box

Credit: Ipsy

Ipsy is one of the OGs in the world of beauty subscription boxes (and the parent brand to another favorite on our list: BoxyCharm). The Glam Bag sends five deluxe-size products each month, based on a beauty quiz taken when you first subscribe. Your subscription also nicely includes a new storage pouch to reuse each month, all for a flat fee of $13 monthly.

Delivery frequency: Monthly

Price: $13 per month

Best Subscription Box for Clean Beauty

The Detox Market The Detox Box

Best Subscription Box for Clean Beauty

The Detox Market The Detox Box

Credit: The Detox Market

Just like all of the skincare, makeup, and body care offerings at the Detox Market, the retailer’s subscription contains clean beauty favorites worthy of any rotation. Past boxes have included picks from True Botanicals, Moon Juice, Ursa Major, Rituel de Fille, and other brands.

Delivery frequency: Monthly, every three months, or every six months

Price: $58.95 for a month, $137.85 for three months, or $239.70 for six months

Best Subscription Box for K-Beauty Lovers

FaceTory Subscription Box

Best Subscription Box for K-Beauty Lovers

FaceTory Subscription Box

Credit: Facetory

Beloved K-beauty brand FaceTory is known for their vibrant, cheekily named sheet masks, which are soaked with skin-loving ingredients. The brand offers two subscription tiers: a monthly option, which includes seven masks and two bonus items; or a quarterly plan, which contains seven masks and four full-size skincare products.

Delivery frequency: Monthly or quarterly

Cost: $20 per month or $50 every three months

Best Subscription Box for TikTok Beauty Fans

Neen Month-to-Month Subscription

Best Subscription Box for TikTok Beauty Fans

Neen Month-to-Month Subscription

Credit: Neen

Neen offers a unique take on the beauty box subscription model. Instead of getting makeup that doesn’t match your color style or skin tone, Neen sends five cards with makeup swatches for you to try. Once you pick which items are most suitable, the subscription cost is transferred into store credit for you to cash in for a full-size product.

Delivery frequency: Monthly, every five months, or every 10 months

Cost: $12 per month, $55 for five months, or $100 for 10 months

Best Subscription Box for Skincare Tools

BeautyBio Blush Box Subscription

Best Subscription Box for Skincare Tools

BeautyBio Blush Box Subscription

Credit: BeautyBio

Fans of BeautyBio’s lineup of effective skincare and high-performance tools will love this box of bestsellers from the brand. The only “catch” is that you won’t know which BeautyBio favorites you’ll get in each delivery, but they’re always valued at $150 or more. The surprise selections arrive every three months.

Delivery frequency: Quarterly

Cost: $75 every three months

Best Subscription Box for Beauty and Wellness Essentials

KND by Kinder Beauty Subscription Box

Best Subscription Box for Beauty and Wellness Essentials

KND by Kinder Beauty Subscription Box

Credit: KND by Kinder Beauty

Not only does KND by Kinder Beauty offer a mix of both beauty and wellness products within its subscription—including supplements, face serums, and fragrances—but its quarterly delivery frequency also gives you a little breathing room for product empties before the next box arrives. Since the brand prioritizes clean and vegan staples, it’s also a great choice for anyone looking to streamline their routines.

Delivery frequency: Quarterly

Cost: $65 every four months

Best Perfume Subscription Box

Scentbird Subscription Box

Best Perfume Subscription Box

Scentbird Subscription Box

Credit: Scentbird

Scentbird is ideal for perfume lovers in search of a new favorite scent. The brand’s catalog of fragrances include a number of holy-grail favorites, like Prada’s Candy and Marc Jacobs’s Daisy, and arrive in a branded atomizer case that holds roughly 120 sprays. Subscribers choose which eight-milliliter sample they want to try with each delivery, which makes rotating your seasonal fragrances, easy.

Delivery frequency: Monthly

Cost: $17 per month

Best Subscription Box for Full-Size Products

BoxyCharm Subscription Box

Best Subscription Box for Full-Size Products

BoxyCharm Subscription Box

Credit: Boxycharm

BoxyCharm’s beauty subscription comes stacked with five full-size products each month from beloved brands like Glow Recipe, Tarte, and Too Faced. Customers can nicely choose three of the five products that arrive in their box each month. The brand even has a storefront where shoppers can purchase other must-have favorites for up to 80 percent off typical retail prices.

Delivery frequency: Monthly

Price: $28/month

Best Subscription Box for Indie Brands

Tribe Beauty Box Bimonthly Subscription

Best Subscription Box for Indie Brands

Tribe Beauty Box Bimonthly Subscription

Credit: Tribe Beauty Box

Inside Tribe’s bimonthly beauty box, you’ll discover top products from primarily indie and woman-owned makeup brands. It’s an ideal mix for makeup lovers looking to discover brands that otherwise may not make it to their FYP. Plus, since each item included comes full-size, you’ll definitely get bang for your buck.

Delivery frequency: Bimonthly

Cost: $40 every two months

Best Tried-and-True Subscription Box

Birchbox Customizable Subscription Box

Best Tried-and-True Subscription Box

Birchbox Customizable Subscription Box

Credit: Birchbox

It’s not a stretch to say Birchbox helped pave the way in the beauty subscription box space. Customers choose exactly what goes into their boxes, based on select deluxe- and travel-size offerings from well-known brands, like Sunday Riley, Supergoop!, and Kiehl’s. Shoppers can also purchase full-size beauty products directly from the Birchbox site.

Delivery frequency: Monthly, quarterly, or annually

Cost: $20 per month, $57 for a quarterly subscription, or $204 for an annual subscription

Best for Acne-Prone Skin

Curology Starter Set

Best for Acne-Prone Skin

Curology Starter Set

Credit: Curology

Curology’s gentle products are made with acne-prone skin concerns and expert-level consultation in mind. Shoppers start with a quiz to determine their skin type and needs, which puts them in touch with a professional dermatologist who creates a unique treatment plan.

Delivery frequency: Monthly

Cost: $30 per month

Best for Manicure Enthusiasts

Orly Color Pass

Best for Manicure Enthusiasts

Orly Color Pass

Credit: Orly

This is truly one of the cutest subscription boxes for nail care lovers. Subscribers gain early access to each of the brand’s newest collections before they officially drop, and at 50 percent off normal retail costs. Plus, each box comes with bonus nail surprises from Orly.

Delivery frequency: Quarterly or monthly

Cost: $36 for a quarterly subscription or $119 for an annual subscription

Best for Olive & June Superfans

Olive & June The Olive You Club

Best for Olive & June Superfans

Olive & June The Olive You Club

Credit: Olive & June

Olive & June’s yearly subscription contains all the nail essentials needed to pull off the perfect at-home mani or pedi, including their top-rated polish. And as a bonus, Olive You Club subscribers also get 10 percent off on all box systems and free shipping, in addition to racking up brand points to use all year.

Delivery frequency: Customizable

Cost: $40 per year
