Short Bio

The Vermont native Lucas Congdon was born in the early 1980s to Jane Werley, who helps Lucas as Master Stonemason in Lucas Lagoon. He belongs to American nationality and holds white ethnicity.

Nothing much is known about his father. However, Lucas inherited the gene of exterior designing from his family, as they worked as landscapers and stonemason. He started as an amateur designer at the age of 14 and used to cut and craft stones.

Lucas went to Montana State University in 1996. Later, he completed his graduation in Landscape Designing in 1997, as per wiki.
