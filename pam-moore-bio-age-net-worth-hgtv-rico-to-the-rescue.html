April 2024 · 5 minute read

HGTV’s Rico to the Rescue isn’t a one-man show. The star Rico León will also be aided by a team of experts including designer Pam Moore and chief estimator Matt Plowman.

So, who’s Pam? Keep reading this Pam Moore Bio to learn all about her.

Pam Moore On HGTV Rico to the Rescue

Pam Moore is the designer on Rico to the Rescue who will be helping the titular star aid a lot of Colorado families having trouble with renovations. But it seems, Pam wouldn’t just be helping Rico to analyze what’s gone wrong in construction projects, but also help him to bring some much-needed diversity to the HGTV show. The designer holds Indian roots in case you didn’t know.

Heavy stated that when Rico to the Rescue premieres, Rico will become HGTV’s only Latino host. Also, they mentioned that 37 primetime shows premiered or returned on HGTV between January 1 and August 31, 2022. That added up to 370 new TV episodes airing on the network during that time — more than any other traditional cable network — yet there were zero Latino on-air leads or co-leads and zero Latino showrunners or directors.

Indeed it was surprising that such a  popular channel failed to incorporate any Latinos into their shows.

For those who can’t wait, in the premiere ep, Pam and her team will meet a distressed couple who are more than a year into their unfinished renovations with a contractor. And thanks to it, the relationship has turned nasty! Upset with the builder’s poor communication and project management skills, Rico will attempt to mediate the situation — but with no success!

According to Rico, he aims to be an advocate for families who desperately need someone with empathy and experience to “turn the construction nightmare into a dream home.” He future explained that whenever got a call for help, his goal was to resolve the conflict between the homeowner and the home builder and to go above and beyond to complete the project.

How Much Is Pam Moore Net Worth?

Pam Moore flaunted a net worth of above $1 million in 2022.

This designer has been in the industry for over 17 years! Right after graduating with a bachelor’s degree in interior design from the Art Institutes in 2005, Pam went on to start Poonam’s By Design, LLC and she never looked back.

Now, the firm originally started in Aurora, Colorado also serves Denver and the greater Colorado area. “We bring unique and creative ideas to each interior design project. Along with our client’s passion and style, every project comes to life. Our goal is to stir up creativity and enthusiasm in each of our clients and expose them to design,” their bio wrote.

Also, they claimed to manage every aspect of the interior design process to ensure the finished project met their standards and surpassed their client’s expectations. And this was also what set them apart from others — their art form. Be it of any budget or taste, their designs were unparalleled.

Their services include Kitchen Interior Design & Remodel, Bathroom Interior Design & Remodel, Basement Interior Design & Build Out, Remodel & Renovations Design to Build Out, Carpeting Selection & Installation, Cabinetry/Closets, Professional Installation, Hardwood Flooring, and so on.

You can also shop their Accessories, Chandeliers, Furshishing, Lamps, Mirrors, and Pendants online at “poonamsbydesign.com.”

Is Pam Moore Married?

Yes, as of 2022, Pam Moore is married to her husband Everett Moore for over 26 years. The two tied the knot back on January 12, 1996.

“Happy 23rd Anniversary to the love of my life!  You simply complete me! Love you so much more than words can ever describe. Cheers to us and to our blessed future ahead baby,” Pam posted a series of FB pics wishing her husband on their 23rd anniversary.

Her husband, Everett is the owner and CEO of Class Transportation, a luxury transportation company available to discerning clients in the Denver metro area. 

Everett turned 50 on June 23, 2022. Wishing him a good day, Pam took to her FB to write, “Facebook Fam, Help me wish my guy, my amazing hubby/boyfriend, Everett a big happy birthday!  Happy birthday, baby!  May this be your best year yet! Let’s do this! Love you so much!”

As for their kids, Pam and Everett have three daughters named Jazmin, Jayda, and Ashley. Among them, Ashley Fenton-Sanders is the eldest and probably Everett’s child from his previous husband.

Ashley turned 30 on March 12, 2022. While Jazmin’s just hit 24 on June 1, 1998. 

Today, Jazmin’s a Second Grade Teacher at Success Academy Charter Schools. She has a Master’s in Business Administration degree from New York University.

As for Jayda, she was into fashion design. In 2022, she lived in  Aurora, Colorado with her boyfriend  Carrington Duhon.
