Mashirima Kapombe, Son Step out Looking Stunning as She Celebrates Her 35th Birthday

July 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Mashirima Kapombe turned 35 and took to social media to celebrate herself as she started a new year in style
  • She posted a photo of herself and her son and declared her new year to be full of peace, love, success and joy
  • Her fellow celebrities, colleagues and netizens flocked to her post to celebrate her and wish her a happy birthday

Citizen TV News anchor Mashirima Kapombe is celebrating her 35th birthday, and she has penned a sweet message in her honour.

The mother of one stepped out looking dope as she posed for a photo with her son, who was smiling next to her.

Mashirima Kapombe's wishes for her 35th year

She looked regal in a long red sleeveless silk dress and paired it with a black top and necklaces, while her son wore navy blue denim jeans, a black top and a grey coat.
