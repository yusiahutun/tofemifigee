Beyonces most fashionable maternity looks

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Beyonce’s twins are being dressed in style before they have even been born – see the singer’s most stylish maternity looks here...

    Beyonce’s wardrobe is the envy of many, and now that she is dressing for three, she has taken her style to a whole new level. She may be carrying twins, but Queen B sure knows how to dress a bump. The star has showcased plenty of head-turning ensembles of late, spanning form-fitting dresses at red carpet events to cool jeans and bang-on-trend slogan tees while spending time relaxing at home.

    From pastel shades to glitzy numbers, mini-dresses and knee high boots to floor-length gowns, take a look at the soon-to-be mother-of-three’s very best maternity looks…

    Beyonce dazzled on stage at the Grammys in a head-turning gold embellished Peter Dundas gown to debut her baby bump back in February. The regal look was accented with an incredible gilded crown - after all, she is Queen B. 

    Who could forget Beyonce's pregnancy announcement outfit? Kneeling before a stunning flower arrangement wearing a long, lime-toned veil, she channelled mother earth as she clutched her blossoming bump. 

    A statement slogan tee is even more appropriate when it speaks volumes. We loved Beyonce's 'preggers' t-shirt, which she teamed with ripped jeans, a denim jacket and white trainers while posing in her garden. The stylish mother-to-be accessorised with sophisticated oversized shades.  

    At the Inaugural Wearable Art Gala in Los Angeles earlier this month, Beyonce worked a fashion-forward look to flaunt her growing bump. Her red form-fitting dress by designer Stephanie Costello was paired with an embellished headdress adorned with beautiful flowers and butterflies - wow. 

    Last month, Queen B continued to nail maternity dressing, posting a photograph on Instagram wearing a stylish, loose-fitting dress that draped effortlessly over her bump. The ensemble was teamed with a pair of knee-high boots and statement shades.  

    The star worked one of this season's major fashion trends beautifully this week, mixing her patterns with ultimate style. She teamed a pastel pink blouse with a delicate lilac-hued blazer adorned with a floral motif. A cool bag and ripped jeans gave her look the perfect finish. 

    A powder-pink jacket, figure-hugging dress and gold heels worked their magic as Beyonce posed in an envy-inducing outfit on Instagram last month. The question is, was she trying to tell us something about her twins with the ultra-girly colour choice?

    How beautiful does Beyonce look here? This stunning long-sleeved purple gown delicately covered her bump and accentuated her enviable curves. The look was accessorised with a pair of statement gold earrings. 

    Beyonce certainly knows how to rock a black and gold look. The fashionable singer worked a suitably fashion-forward maternity ensemble on Instagram this week, teaming a form-fitting black mini dress with a pair of patent heels, hat and 'LOVED' embroidered Gucci bag with a stunning gold silk drape jacket. 

    The pregnant star posted a second picture of the on-trend outfit, of which saw her add extra style points with a pair of oversized statement shades. 

    Beyonce looked cool and relaxed in a semi-sheer black blouse and her staple ripped boyfriend jeans, accessorised with Miu Miu shades and a Gucci bag with the slogan 'LOVED' embroidered across it. The outfit was completed with a pair of studded sandals, pretty braids and a vibrant red lipstick.
