Best Perfume Gift Sets For Her 2024

February 2024 · 7 minute read

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Baccarat Rouge 540 Gift Set

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Baccarat Rouge 540 Gift Set

Few fragrances have successfully captured the zeitgeist of today like Baccarat Rouge 540 by Maison Francis Kurkdjian – the house of trailblazing master perfumer Francis Kurkdjian. For fans of the heady and moreish gourmand scent, this makes the ultimate gift. Comprising the eau de parfum in 70ml, its accompanying 250ml Body Lotion, and the 280g Baccarat Rouge 540 scented candle, it's drips grownup glamour.

Chanel No5 Eau de Toilette Purse Spray

Chanel No5 Eau de Toilette Purse Spray

Credit: Boots

Take your pick from Chanel's iconic fragrance family – you really can't go wrong. The most fail-safe choice, however, has to be No5. It's elegance in a bottle (or, rather, in a chic refillable travel case).

Tom Ford Private Blend Fucking Fabulous gift set

Tom Ford Private Blend Fucking Fabulous gift set

Always taking fragrance to opulent new heights, Tom Ford's provocative private blends are beyond addictive. Consisting of an eau de parfum of the leather and lavender-enthused elixir and an atomiser for on-the-go spritizing, this set (as much as the scent) is aptly named.

Jo Loves A Fragrance Paintbrush™ Collection

Jo Loves A Fragrance Paintbrush™ Collection

Jo Malone CBE is a leader in fragrance innovation, and we love her Fragrance Paintbrushes which allow you to sweep her vibrant Jo Loves scents onto the skin. This gift set – including 7ml A Fragrance Paintbrush versions of No. 42 The Flower Shop, Pomelo, Green Orange & Coriander, White Rose & Lemon Leaves and Jo By Jo Loves (the fragrance she created for herself) – is a deliciously different way to gift perfume.

Liberty LBTY. Fragrance Discovery Set

Liberty LBTY. Fragrance Discovery Set

One of the most exciting launches in beauty last year was LBTY., the first fragrance line by storied departmental store, Liberty. The five-piece collection – each inspired by heritage prints – has been a huge success, but if you're yet to try and buy it, this discovery set is a wise move. The trio of eau de parfums includes Tudor, Wild Rosinda and Liberty Maze which smell as punchy as their accompanying prints suggest.

Diptyque Eau de Parfum Discovery Set

Diptyque Eau de Parfum Discovery Set

Experience Maison Diptyque with this chic set of five fragrances, from the floral, to the spicy and woody. Philosykos, Do Son, Fleur de Peau, Orphéon and Eau Rose all feature in this generous collection, allowing your loved ones to explore the expertly crafted world of Diptyque one spritz at a time.

Victoria Beckham Beauty Fragrance Discovery Set

Victoria Beckham Beauty Fragrance Discovery Set

Victoria Beckham Beauty's first foray into fragrance was met with much fanfare in 2023, and we've been completely seduced by the three scents as much as the intimate stories behind them. The discovery set makes the perfect present for a lover; each fragrance aims to capture Victoria's recollections of times and places – all of which speak to her romantic journey with David. We'd gift it with the promise of a full size version of the recipient's favourite.

Maison Margiela Replica Memory Box

Maison Margiela Replica Memory Box

Maison Margiela's cult Replica collection bottles moments in time and the memories we associate with them. This set of 10 minis will transport you to a Lazy Sunday Morning, Beach Walk, Bubble Bath or Springtime In A Park. For winter, though, By The Fireplace is perfect. Warming from the opening note, red berries and chestnut provoke a romantic ambiance, while clove oil and cashmere notes provide a warm base of crackling wood burning. It's the perfect year-long gift for a fragrance fan.

Acqua di Parma Blu Mediterraneo Miniature Set

Acqua di Parma Blu Mediterraneo Miniature Set

Now 14% Off

Anyone who veers towards bright citrus, floral and woody scents will adore this perfume gift set. It features five of Acqua di Parma's most beloved scents: Arancia di Capri, Bergamotto di Calabria, Fico di Amalfi, Mirto di Panarea and Mandorlo di Sicilia.

Parfums de Marly Delina Trio Eau de Parfum Fragrance Gift Set

Parfums de Marly Delina Trio Eau de Parfum Fragrance Gift Set

For any rose perfume fans, look no further: this coffret from the elegant Parfums de Marly features the brand's three signature scents, all centred around a heart of Turkish rose. Contemporary versions on the theme, they're fresh, feminine and flirtatious – plus they look very pretty to boot.

Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium, Mon Paris and Libre Gift Set

Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium, Mon Paris and Libre Gift Set

This stylish trifecta of hero scents has everything you need for a stunningly perfumed party season. A guaranteed crowd pleaser, this decadent collection contains 10ml sizes of YSL's bestselling scents: Black Opium, a sultry gourmand, Libre, a grand orange blossom floral, and Mon Paris, a romantic fruity chypre.

Dries Van Noten Selection Quai Malaquais Discovery set

Dries Van Noten Selection Quai Malaquais Discovery set

If we gave a glimpse into our Bazaar beauty team's most beloved fragrances, you would undoubtedly find a Dries Van Noten or two. These high fashion scents are not only daring and unique, their vibrant packaging mean they'll always hold pride of place on our vanities. This handbag friendly selection of miniature mists are an effortless introduction to the brand, not to mention their adorable vials mimic the full-size designs. Featuring Santal Greenery, Voodoo Chile, Roch The Myrrh, Fleur Du Mal and Soie Malaquais.

Le Labo Discovery Set

Le Labo Discovery Set

From Santal 33 and Another 13 to Thé Matcha 26 and Noir 29, Le Labo's unique olfactive craftsmanship requires little introduction. This fine unisex fragrance sets contains six of the brand's most enticing scents, all instantly recognisable with a signature Le Labo label.

Sana Jardin Mystical Spray Trio Gift Set

Sana Jardin Mystical Spray Trio Gift Set

Founded by a former social worker, Sana Jardin sets the standard when it comes to socially responsible perfumery. From business training to ensuring year-round employment, the Moroccan flower harvesters it works with are safeguarded from the seasonality of the industry.

But that's not the only reason to give the brand's chic gift set this year – these fragrances, including the calming softness of Sandalwood Temple, the sparkling mood-booster Berber Blonde and the warmly seductive Tiger By Her Side, are all exquisite.

Christian Louboutin Loubiworld Miniature Fragrance Gift Set

Christian Louboutin Loubiworld Miniature Fragrance Gift Set

Credit: Harrods

Christian Louboutin's perfume gift set is made with magpies in mind. The instantly recognisable red lacquered box opens to reveal seven ornate, pleasingly heavy bottles, each one topped with a filigree cap that reflects the feel of the fragrance. From the leathery Loubiraj to the rose-tinted Loubidoo, they're bound to select a new signature.

Molton Brown Woody & Aromatic Fragrance Discovery Set

Molton Brown Woody & Aromatic Fragrance Discovery Set

Molton Brown's timeless scents are a classic gifting choice, and this sleek set contains three heady headliners: Re-charge Black Pepper, Coastal Cypress & Sea Fennel, and Mesmerising Oudh Accord & Gold. All three are distinctive, yet share the spirit that comes with woody, aromatic accords.

D.S. & Durga Big City Jams Discovery Set

D.S. & Durga Big City Jams Discovery Set

Memorable without being challenging, D.S & Durga crafts the kind of niche fragrances we can really get behind. This striking discovery set contains vials of six of the brand's evocative perfumes, including Coriander, Burning Barbershop, Bistro Waters, Grapefruit Generation, Notorious Oud and Italian Citrus. It's ideal for sampling, sharing, and finding your new signature scent.

House of Whind Fragrance Discovery Set

House of Whind Fragrance Discovery Set

Inspired by its founders native to Morocco – in celebration of Arab beauty rituals with a contemporary twist – Whind is a brand like no other. As are its fragrances. Each expertly blended by master perfumer Jerome Epinette, this discovery set elegantly evokes the mystery of Morocco.
