Heather Rabun Age

Heather Rabun Job

As per LinkedIn, Heather Rabun is an experienced choreographer and non-profit co-founder with a demonstrated history of working in the philanthropy and arts/entertainment industry.

Since May 2004, Heather has been working as a freelance choreographer at her own company named Heather Rabun.

In between, she also worked as a dance specialist at Allegra Dance Greenwich; as a group fitness instructor at Pure Barre; co-founder, and B.E. dance LA creator at Beautify Earth.

Her working experience includes live concert dance performances, musical theater, live event entertainment, debutante balls, “Dancing with the Stars” ballroom competitions, audition movement, and wedding dances. Her choreography in Allegro Scratch, a short film produced by Cindy Quijada, earned the titles of “Best Dance Short Film” at Riurau Film Festival 2017 and “Gold Winner in Choreography” in the International Independent Film Awards 2017.

Heather, aka “Dancin Heather”, is also known for dancing while painting. She has danced professionally for companies including L.A. Choreographers & Dancers and Dallas Neo-Classical Ballet.
