TikTok Human Dog Bed on Amazon 2024

April 2024 · 2 minute read

If you've ever looked at your dog's bed and thought that it seemed like a super cozy place to take a nap, we promise you're not going crazy. There's a human-sized dog bed for sale on Amazon and TikTok users are obsessed with it (it was even featured on Shark Tank!). Trust us—this massive pillow-y creation is going to be your favorite place to snooze or read, with or without your pet.

Plufl The Original Human Dog Bed

The bed is about five and a half feet long and nearly three feet wide, giving you plenty of space to curl up and get cozy. A bolster curves around the body creating a comforting, snuggled feel, and also acts as a head and footrest if you want to stretch out a bit more.

It comes with a vegan faux fur shell that is seriously soft and luxe (just make sure to air dry after machine-washing to keep it ultra-plush). Full disclosure, my teenager got one and not only naps in this, but sometimes opts to spend the night sleeping here as it is just as comfortable (if not more so!) than her actual bed.

One reviewer says: "I use it everyday and it replaced my couch! I would highly recommend buying this product because it will be the best nap you ever take! Even my dog and I fight over who gets to use it!"

While this product is not exactly cheap, you can justify the cost because it can also be used as a guest bed instead of an air mattress when you have overnight visitors (your friends will be delighted). And also because this Plufl is currently discounted 25%, making it $299 instead of the usual $399.
