Biography

Liz Vassey was born in Raleigh, NC on August 9, 1972. Best known during the late 1980s and early 1990s as Emily Ann Sago on the popular ABC soap opera All My Children, this American actress also played the role of Wendy Simms on CBS’s CSI: Crime Scene Investigation. She appeared with Eliza Dushku on several episodes of the Fox series Tru Calling. Early in her career, she portrayed Sylvia in the 1993 Jason Priestly film Calendar Girl.
