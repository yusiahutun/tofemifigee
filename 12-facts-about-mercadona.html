Mercadona is a popular Spanish supermarket chain.

Mercadona, known for its high-quality products and competitive prices, is one of the largest and most successful supermarket chains in Spain.

Mercadona was founded in 1977 by Juan Roig.

Juan Roig, the owner and president of Mercadona, started the company with a small butcher shop in Valencia. It has since grown into a nationwide retail giant.

Mercadona has over 1,600 stores across Spain.

With its extensive network of stores, Mercadona provides convenient access to a wide range of products for millions of customers throughout the country.
