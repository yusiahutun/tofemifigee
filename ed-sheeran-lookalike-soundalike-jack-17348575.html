Ginger ringer: Ed Sheeran doppelgnger looks and sounds like the singer-songwriter

June 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Not only does Jack Shepherd look like the Thinking Out Loud star, but he sounds like him too.

In fact, the uncanny resemblance is so striking that the 23-year-old is mobbed in the street by fans and is even set to fill in for the chart-topping star at an upcoming wedding.

Jack, from Reading, has been performing for the past four years and has carved a successful career out of his similar appearance - and voice - to Ed.

And with Ed Sheeran topping the charts on both sides of the Atlantic with his 2014 album' X' - Jack's career has skyrocketed too.

Jack said: "In the early days when A-team first came out I kept getting told I looked like Ed, especially with the ginger hair.

"And when I did gigs I always played 'Drunk' and people would tell me how much I sounded like him - but that's just the way I sing, I don't try to sing like him.

"I always get mistaken for Ed - it doesn't matter where I go - and the best is when fans sing Ed Sheeran songs at me."

Jack, who also performs alone using a loop pedal, is regularly mobbed by fans asking for photos and autographs - and even more strangely, someone even popped the question

The musician takes it all in his stride and said he feels very lucky to have a good fan base of people who know every word to all of Sheeran's songs.

But, despite his success as an impersonator, Jack has never met his celebrity double.

He said: "People always stop me and ask for photographs, it's an everyday occurrence, and I am more than happy to pose with them.

"It's a great thing to be able to make someone's day, especially with younger fans who actually think I am Ed Sheeran.

"I have had some very strange requests and even a marriage proposal.

"One time Ed was playing in Reading just around the corner from the pub I was in and I was bombarded by people - I ended up having to sneak out the back. It was so surreal."

"This has been the craziest time of my life. I haven't had the chance to meet Ed yet, but it would be great to. Just to find out if he likes what I do."

As the UK's number one Ed Sheeran tribute act, Jack is inundated with requests and bookings for him to perform.

And Jack got perhaps the ultimate compliment when a couple asked him to perform at their big day - after the real Ed had to pull out.
