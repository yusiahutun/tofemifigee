No matter how tough a situation is, a person can always get out of it and build his/her life over. An excellent example of such a circumstance is the life of American rapper Quando Rondo.

He grew up in a neighborhood prone to crimes & gang activities and even spent a big part of his teens in juvenile detention centers. Naturally gifted at creating music, Quando later focused on music to pursue a career and build his life away from the injustice of society.

Parents, Drug Addict Mother

Quando Rondo (born: Tyquian Terrel Bowman) - a native of Savannah, Georgia - was born on 23 March 1999. He belonged to a family with very unfavorable conditions as his father was imprisoned most of the time, and his mother was a drug addict. Hence, he was placed for adoption.

Sadly, due to the harmful influences of the crime-prone area he grew up in, Quando was caught continuously in gang violence.

Due to his actions, he became a constant visitor at the juvenile detention centers. During the detention, the artist of height 5 feet 8 inches (1.73 m) began rapping and figured that he was good at the craft. So, he decided to grind his skills to make his career out of music. 

Relationship Status, Girlfriend?

Quando’s fans know about his incident with girlfriend Jai Doll, which happened in March 2019. The pair were living together for about six months until the artist caught Jai sleeping with his cousin. As soon as he found the betrayal, Quando threw Jai Doll out of his apartment. The two had been dating for about a year before the incident.

Quando Rondo left his ex-girlfriend Jai Doll after she cheated on him (Photo: Youtube.com)

Jai Claimed that she slept with Quando’s cousin while they were “on a break,” but Quando did not listen to any explanations his now ex-girlfriend provided.

Thus, the relationship between Jai and Quando ended. As of October 2019, Quando is single and does not seem to be involved in any potential romance.

Career & Net Worth

Quando’s career started in 2017 when he shared bits of his songs on Soundcloud. Some of his songs went viral, helping him gain a lot of followers on Instagram. As time passed, he started getting recognized by prominent artists like The Game, Birdman, and YoungBoy Never Broke Again. Eventually, Quando was able to sign a deal with Atlantic Records.

In 2018, Quando collaborated with Lil Baby and released a song titled I Remember which ended up becoming his breakthrough track. The song went on to gain over 11 million views on YouTube. Shortly after, Quando came up with another song called Motivation. The single, too, earned a lot of attention.

And, in April of 2018, Quando’s mixtape called Life B4 Fame hit the streets. It featured many artists such as Tec, OMB Peezy, and Lil Durk. The 11-track mixtape gained over 2 million streams in just two days. His album eventually achieved 6 million downloads changing Quando’s whole career.

Following up on his success, Quando released his second mixtape with the name Life After Fame, five months after his first mixtape. The tape featured several artists like Youngboy Never Broke Again, Rich Homie Quan, and more.

His most recent songs include Rich Homie Quando and Kiccin Sh_t.
