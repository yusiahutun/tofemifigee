The 52 Best Luxury Gifts for Men of 2024

September 2024 · 23 minute read

Men who appreciate the finer things in life are notoriously difficult to shop for because if they like something, they’ve probably already bought it for themselves. But here’s the thing: The world is filled with exceptional finds, from the smoky, fruity, chocolatey goodness of top-shelf whiskey to the thunderous bass of high-fi streaming speakers, from the supple leather of an Italian-made wallet to the incredible plushness of California’s finest surfer-approved sweatpants. And you don’t have to be a multi-millionaire or an oil baron to enjoy them. Even if your luxury man thinks he’s got it all, we’re here with the list of 52 lavish must-haves that he didn’t know he wanted — till now.

Bleusalt Classic Button-Up

Bleusalt

Monday feels like Sunday in this all-around perfect men’s collared shirt from Malibu-based Bleusalt. The look is pure profesh—top-stitched double breast pockets, double button cuffed sleeves, elbow patches, and abalone shell buttons, but the feel is all leisure, thanks to stretchy-soft Tencel modal with spandex. Reviewers give it five stars across the board—one woman proclaims: “Hubby’s favorite shirt!” Comes in six sizes and seven colors—we’re loving classic navy.

Kizik Men’s Vegas

Kizik

Leather sneaks are the best thing to ever happen to business casual, and this style from Kizik is the best of the genre. Full-grain leather on top and rabbit foam outsoles on the bottom means he’s basically walking on stylish pillows—to a big meeting, the big game, or a big night out. As a bonus: The insoles are removable and washable, so it’s fine if he wears them every single day. Sizing goes up to 15. Also comes in ivory white, black (with black sole), pebble gray, chestnut and blush pink.

Mizu Meditation Candle Discovery Set of 6

Mizu

For guys who meditate or just appreciate a soul-soothing scent, Rhode Island-based Mizu has a set of six clean, vegan candles from their Meditation Collection. Packaged in eco-friendly glass jars and made with 100% natural ingredients—essential oils, organic extracts, vegetable waxes, the scents feel sophisticated and refined, calming and grounding. Some of our favorites are Lavender Pine (with hints of mint), Palo Santo (like an Ecuadorean forest), Desert Juniper (like desert winds with juniper berries, cedar and laurel life) and Hinoki (like fresh mountain air, with cypress and grapefruit).

Isamu Noguchi Chess Set

MoMa Design Store

He need not be a grandmaster to want to make his chess game more artful. This modern chess set, a newly authorized edition originally designed in 1944 by Japanese-American sculptor Isamu Noguchi, is made of mini abstract plexiglass figurines — the tallest, the King, is three inches high — on a folding board. When it’s not chess night, display this modern marvel as an extraordinary conversation piece. Checkmate!

Spiceology Set of 12 Gourmet BBQ Spice Rubs

Amazon

Dry spice rubs are a simple way to kick up the flavor of grilled, seared, roasted or broiled fish or meat, and this collection of bestsellers from chef-owned Spiceology has kick in abundance. For brisket, we like to use Chef Chad White’s Brisket Rub (hint: it also creates the “bark” you want); for a rack of ribs on the grill, we like Cowboy Crust Espresso Chile Rub to add coffee and smoky flavors; we like to add Greek Freak to veggies for Mediterranean flare. Bonus: Unlike traditional heavy BBQ sauces, the spice blends have zero calories.

Ghurka 5 Center Stitch Belt

Ghurka

A well-made leather belt is the difference between looking fine and looking fine. We love this 1.5-inch wide vintage leather belt with brass roller buckle from artisanal leather workshop Ghurka. Handmade in the USA, it’s simple and elegant and says, “I look good.” Comes in three wardrobe-essential colors, sizes 34 to 42 (order two sizes up for the best fit).

Mizzen + Main Leeward No Tuck Dress Shirt

Mizzen + Main

Post-covid, business dress codes have relaxed a bit, but your man still needs to look professional and polished. Enter the No Tuck dress shirt from Mizzen + Main. Two inches shorter than regular length, it’s meant to be worn loose and casual, though it’s still a flattering trim fit. (Don’t worry, it’s also a bit stretchy, with 12% spandex.) Of the 11 styles, from solids to plaids, we’re loving the White Geo Floral pattern—a manly take tiny flowers for a man who knows who he is. Try the City Flannel version on weekends. Comes in S to XXL.

Alpha Industries B-3 Sherpa Leather Bomber Jacket

Made in the USA from 100 percent sheepskin leather, it’s lined in sherpa wool fleece to withstand extreme cold weather, and it has stylish buckle straps on the collar and hem to ensure a perfect fit. It might be the most comfortable, cool jacket he’s ever put on his body — and he may never want to take it off.
