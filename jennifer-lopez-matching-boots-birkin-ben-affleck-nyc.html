Jennifer Lopez Matches Her Tall Boots to Her Herms Birkin in NYC

June 2024 · 2 minute read

“Another day, another Hermès Birkin” seems to be a mantra of sorts for Jennifer Lopez. And on her latest outing, she appeared to be test-driving a new style formula: matching her boots to her luxurious bag.

On Friday, the “On the Floor” hitmaker and husband Ben Affleck stepped out for a low-key outing in New York City—where Lopez showed thateven the most laid-back occasions can benefit from some glam.

Gotham//Getty Images

The Hustlers star cut a stylish figure in a long wool taupe coat that featured a dramatic collar with wide lapels, four small black buttons down the middle, and a hidden, built-in belt that she knotted in the middle. She paired the outerwear, which she appeared to be treating like a dress, with tall pointed-toe leather stiletto boots and one of her enviable Hermès treasures—both in a rich chocolate brown. (The Birkin was a glossy, crocodile-embossed piece, accented with shiny gold hardware.) Lopez’s masterful color coordination didn’t stop there, as she added a pair of oversize brown tortoiseshell shades with yellow-tinted lenses, along with thin gold hoops. She appeared to be wearing minimal makeup and swept her caramel-highlighted hair back into a sleek ponytail.

As for the Gone Girl actor, he went casual in an ocean-blue T-shirt layered under a navy blue wool coat, paired with dark-wash jeans and his prized pair of Travis Scott’s Nike SB Dunk Low sneakers.

Lopez has been accenting many of her latest fits with her collection of glorious bags. Earlier this month, she put her maximalist style on display in a slate-gray sweatsuit, pale pink running sneakers, a fluffy beige fur coat, and an icy-white Birkin. Days before, she arrived at her dance studio in a black turtleneck, matching yoga pants, a long beige coat, white sneakers, and the same chocolate-brown Birkin. Before then, she elevated a dark gray sweatsuit and short Ugg boots with a rare matte cream Niloticus Crocodile Birkin.
