'Peep on a Perch' Is the New 'Elf on a Shelf'

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Move over, Elf on a Shelf—there's a new holiday toy in town.

This year, the Peep On a Perch is the must-have gift in your kids' Easter baskets. Much like its Christmasy counterpart, Peep On a Perch is a storybook set for children that comes with a plush toy chick and can be enjoyed long before Easter Sunday. The picture book tells the tale of how the Peep helps the Easter Bunny every year to make a special celebration for kids around the world.

Peep On a Perch

Peep On a Perch

According to the story, the sweet Peep helps decorate eggs, fills baskets and—most importantly—can see all the acts of kindness that kids do in the days and weeks leading up to Easter.

Parents can perch the Peeps Chick in various spots around their home to 'watch over' kids during the Lent season. Children will learn that the Peep is noticing their good behavior, from practicing polite manners to helping with chores. As the story goes, the more kindness the Peep sees, the happier the Peep gets. Its purpose is to encourage kids to behave, and help them understand that the holidays are about more than just receiving gifts.

Peep on a Perch is now available for purchase—and it's on sale right now!
