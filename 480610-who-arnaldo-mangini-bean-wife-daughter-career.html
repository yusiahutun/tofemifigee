Early life

The personality was born on 4 December 1973 (age 49 as of 2022) in Rome, Italy. However, he has never mentioned anything about his parents. But he mentions he has a sister whom he has never revealed her name. Mangini spent most of his childhood between Italy and the Netherlands.

His parents lived in Rome until he turned five. Then, Arnaldo Mangini’s family moved to Zwolle in the Netherlands, where he spent six years of his childhood. Despite being young, he accompanied his father to coffee shops and clubs in the Netherlands, learning more about cultural differences.

When Arnaldo turned 11, his parents returned to Italy and lived in Stiava, where he also attended middle school. But from an early age, Arnaldo had a profound interest in comedy. He would stage shows in his home with his sister and force his relatives to attend.

His talent from a young age forced him to attend Liceo Artistico, a secondary school in Italy. Then, he enrolled at Accademia di Belle Arti di Carrara (Carrara Academy of Fine Arts), a public tertiary academy of art in Carrara, in Tuscany, Italy. After his studies at the institution, he ventured into comedy.

Professional career

Before getting a footing in the entertainment industry, Arnaldo Mangini’s first job was as a waiter. Then, in the mid-1990s, he ventured into the world of comedy. In 1996, he went for a TV audition at the Zelig in Milan. After getting considered, his career led him to appear in many television broadcasts, including Rai, Mediaset, and Sky.

He subsequently left TV to venture into theatre and comics. He started his show titled The Arnaldo Mangini Show. The show allowed him to travel throughout Europe to showcase his similarities with and emulate Mr Bean. But his emulation of Mr Bean almost got him in trouble in 2001 with Rowan Atkinson’s team. He worked for Telecom and recorded four TV commercials.

He was not Mr Bean in the commercials and dressed like a doctor. However, his resemblance to Mr Bean caused ripples when Rowan Atkinson’s management sued Telecom and sent Mangini a warning stating that he was damaging the original Mr Bean’s image since the public could not distinguish between the copy and the original. Nevertheless, he continued with his work as a comic.

In 2020, he starred in L’uomo samargantico, portraying Il messaggero. Arnaldo then took his comedy to the next level when he joined social media channels. He started with YouTube, where he uploaded comedy videos. His channel received many subscribers because people thought he looked like Mr Bean. And when TikTok became a reality, Arnaldo started uploading short videos on the platform.

He instantly became a viral hit, particularly for his resemblance to Mr Bean. As of October 2022, Arnaldo Mangini is the second-most followed user on TikTok in Italy, with over 27 million followers. Arnaldo also wrote a book to get across to his increasing number of followers. His book is Il Potere Del Sorriso, translated to The Power of Smiles.
