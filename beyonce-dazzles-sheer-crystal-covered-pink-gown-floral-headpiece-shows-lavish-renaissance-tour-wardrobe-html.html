Beyonce showed off some of the incredible looks she's worn during her Renaissance tour in a series of images shared to her Instagram on Tuesday.

The Crazy in Love hitmaker, 41, lived up to her Queen Bey moniker in a princess-worthy sheer pink gown lined with crystals that showed off her hourglass figure. 

The gown featured an elaborate matching pink floral headpiece and elbow-length gloves with sparkling sequins.

The songstress — who was recently accused by Erykah Badu of ripping off her style — completed the thrilling ensemble with a pair of large diamond earrings and a sizable yellow diamond ring.

She wore her waist-long honey blonde tresses in a straight style and added a few clips of herself performing onstage in the eye-catching look during her show in East Rutherford, New Jersey.

Beyonce's Renaissance world tour, which features a three-hour disco-themed show in support of her seventh studio album, Renaissance, came five years after her prior On the Run II tour.

It is also her first solo tour since The Formation World Tour in 2016. 

She kicked it off in Stockholm, Sweden, on May 10, and will wrap it up in Kansas City on October 1.

Throughout the tour she's worn custom pieces from a number of designers, including Alexander McQueen, David Koma, Courrèges, Loewe, Coperni, Off-White, Mugler, Gucci, Balmain, Ferragamo, Roksanda and more.

Though the tour has been going smoothly, there have been some hiccups, including the time Beyonce was forced to sing instructions to her team in the middle of a performance after a member of her team forgot to 'turn the fan on' in front of her.

While looking for some relief from the hot weather and stage lights, the powerhouse appeared frustrated as an on-stage fan remained off as she belted out her 2011 track, 1+1.

Despite gesturing several times for someone to turn on the machine, her signals seemingly went unnoticed.

Ultimately, the 32-time Grammy winner subtly sang 'turn the fan on' between verses, which immediately prompted someone behind-the-scenes to do their job.

Previously Beyonce went viral for appearing upset during the final number of her show in Amsterdam after her stage crew missed a cue to help her down from a prop.

In a video, which began spreading rapidly on social media, the mother-of-three can be seen performing atop a silver horse prop as she sings her tune Summer Renaissance.

A fan in the audience recorded her pushing a crew member's hand away as he reaches to help her off the fixture.

She later was filmed mouthing 'Oh, my God!' after moving her microphone away from her mouth.

Beyonce was also accused by singer Erykah Badu of ripping off her style with her outlandish Renaissance outfits.

Legendary singer Badu, 52, who is known for breaking style boundaries, took to Instagram on Monday to slam Beyonce for donning an oversized chrome hat which was similar to one worn by Badu onstage.

Sharing Beyonce's post, which showed the superstar bowing her head in the hat, paired with camouflage boots and a glittery IV sports jersey, Badu wrote: 'Hmmm'.

She then shared a post of herself in a near-identical hat and wrote: 'I guess I'm everybody stylist.'

Badu first garnered style kudos upon breaking out on the late 90s music scene for her chameleon-like adaptations of traditional African dress.
