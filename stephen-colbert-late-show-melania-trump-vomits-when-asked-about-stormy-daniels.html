Stephen Colbert's Melania Trump Nearly Vomits When Asked About Stormy Daniels Scandal

August 2024 · 2 minute read

First Lady Melania Trump has remained mum on the subject of President Donald Trump's alleged affair with porn star Stormy Daniels, but at least her "Late Show" impersonator was willing to open up to Stephen Colbert on the topic Friday.

When the comedian asked "Melania" (Laura Benanti) about Daniels -- who's currently suing POTUS for failure to sign a non-disclosure agreement that allegedly covered up their affair -- she replied, "Stephen, I told you before, I don't care about this woman, Slimey Manholes. My Donald is as loyal as the Golden Retriever he buys his hair from."
