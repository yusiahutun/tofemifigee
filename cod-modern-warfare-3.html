The Lockwood 680 is a weapon featured in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 (2023), it is one of the Shotguns available in the game.

The Lockwood 680 will be available in Modern Warfare 3 with the launch of the game on November 10, 2023, and on Warzone later this year with Season One.

The design of the Lockwood 680 is based the real-life gun Remington 870 MCS.

Here you can find the main information about the weapon in MW3, including all the unlockable attachments and the complete Weapon Blueprints list for the Lockwood 680 available in COD Modern Warfare 3 and COD Warzone.
