Who Is Shea Whigham Wife, Christine Whigham? Her Age, Job

April 2024 · 5 minute read

The Righteous Gemstones star, Shea Whigham isn’t just funny on-screen. The actor is fun to talk to in real life as well. Once, when asked about his favorite meal, Shea said his wife Christine Whigham made a spaghetti bolognese from heaven and it would also be his “death row meal.”

So, who is Shea’s wife who makes exquisite bolognese? Keep reading this Christine Whigham Bio to find out.

Meet Christine Whigham, Shea Whigham Wife

Christine Whigham married Shea Whigham back in 1995. So until now (as of 2023), the lovebirds have been together for over 27 years! Their secret? Love, understanding, communication, and bolognese. Yes, bolognese!

“My wife’s spaghetti bolognese. That’s my death row meal. If I’m dying and they come in, I want a nice Napa Cab and a spaghetti bolognese,” the actor explained. And in case that ever comes to pass, he wants people to know, “He never judged. He never sat in judgment of you.”

Do Shea And Christine Whigham Have Kids?

Yes, Christine and Shea Whigham share four kids.

Amongst them, only their daughter Giorgia Whigham followed in the footsteps of her father. Today, Giorgia is an actress best known for her portrayal of “Kat” in the Netflix Original series 13 Reasons Why and “Beth” in the third season of MTV’s Scream.

Giorgia was born on August 19, 1997, in New York City, NY. She started her career as “Nadia” back in the 2016 short film Pinky. The same year, she also landed her role as “Shannon” in Son of Zorn. Thereafter, she went on to grace TV shows and movies including The Legend of Master Legend (2017), Chance (2017), Sierra Burgess Is a Loser (2018), The Punisher (2019), Into the Dark (2020), Legacies (2021), The Orville (2022), A Little White Lie (2023), and Waco: The Aftermath (2023).

Find Giorgia on IG @giorgia_whigham.

On May 13, 2019, Giorgia took to her IG to feature her mother in a post captioned, “Mama you are everything. I love you love you love you.”

Now, talking about Christine’s sons, we don’t know anything about Gus Whigham, but Marlon Whigham did seem to have dabbled in the entertainment industry. So far, he’s worked as a  Production Assistant for Perry Mason (2020), Hour (2021), and Puppet Quest (2021).

Marlon had yet to make it big in the movie/TV scene. But we’re sure he’ll reach great heights after completing his filmmaking degree from the University of North Carolina School of the Arts in 2024.

“I am an independent filmmaker. I have not gone to film school, I got my film education on the floor as a production assistant, so I know how a set works in a much more practical sense,” he said before.

Marlon’s skill set includes camera operation, editing, cinematography, and directing.

What Is Christine Whigham Maiden Name?

Christine Whigham Family

Christine Whigham comes from a family of five.

Her parents are named Donald H and Lynn K Montgomery. They turned 76 and 71 years of age respectively in 2022.

Sadly, we don’t know much about her father, but her mother, Lynn ran Meemas Boutique in NY.  Her shop offered many of her one-of-a-kind, handmade creations. Lynn said that she specialized in crocheting and knitting — from dolls and blankets to baby clothes, toys, and more. Also, her shop included a wide selection of Christmas Stockings (from Vintage Patterns to colorful motifs and designs).

“I create all pieces in my non-smoking, pet-free home in New York. Items are mindfully wrapped, packaged, and shipped to you from Los Angeles and New York using eco-friendly materials to support sustainability,” Lynn said.

As for her siblings, Amy L Montgomery (born: May 1974) worked as a Nutraceutical Representative, while Kathryn A Rolland (born: Mar 1979) owned Indruk, a WordPress Web Design and Development company.

In 2023, Kathryn aka Kate resided in Rochester, New York with her husband and their two lovely kids.

Trivia: Kathryn’s parents-in-law are named Beth and Frank Whigham.

Christine Whigham Job

Christine Whigham didn’t reveal her job.

On the other hand, her husband, Shea is a popular actor and voiceover artist. You might have seen him as a supporting actor in several projects such as Kong: Skull Island, Silver Linings Playbook, Joker, Fast & Furious franchise, and more.

Recently, Shea joined the voice cast of Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, playing Captain Stacy in a crucial role in Gwen Stacy’s universe. Also, he was one of the few new additions to the cast of The Righteous Gemstones season 3.

Looking back, Shea made his television debut with a small role in an episode of the TV series Ghost Stories in 1997. Thereafter, he went on to land even more supporting roles, and finally in 2000, he got his big break with a prominent role in the war-drama film Tigerland.

Now, the actor has almost 100 movies and TV shows to his name.
