Aaron Taylor-Johnson, 33, gives off big Bond energy for appearance without wife Sam, 57

July 2024 · 3 minute read

    Aaron Taylor-Johnson was suited and booted in monochromatic black to attend a special screening of his new movie at BFI IMAX Waterloo on Monday. 

    The star, 33, joined co-stars Emily Blunt, Ryan Gosling and Hannah Waddingham on the yellow carpet, looking suave in a tailored black suit layered over a fitted black vest. 

    Aaron, who is married to British film director Sam Taylor-Johnson, 57, wore his brunette locks pushed back, rocking a rugged full beard and two gold hoop earrings to complete his relaxed-yet-refined aesthetic. 

    The actor's appearance comes shortly after a media whirlwind had tipped him to play the next James Bond - though the identity of the next 007 hasn't officially been confirmed yet.
