Julian Clary reveals his marriage with long-term partner Ian Mackley in the sweetest way

May 2024 · 2 minute read

    Huge congratulations to Julian Clary! The 57-year-old has confirmed that he has tied the knot with long-term partner Ian Mackley during an intimate ceremony over the weekend. Taking to his Twitter page on Sunday, the presenter shared a sweet photograph which showed him signing the marriage register.

    The much-loved television personality shared the happy news with a tongue in cheek message to delighted fans. The snap sees the couple dressed in formal suits with floral corsages after sharing their vows.
