Meatball Molly's UFC London opponent Hannah Goldy is OnlyFans star who posts nude snaps

September 2024 · 2 minute read

'Meatball' Molly McCann is gearing up to continue her hot streak when she faces Hannah '24k' Goldy at UFC London next month.

McCann commanded the attention of the world when she dropped Luana Carolina with a spinning back elbow in under two minutes. The win took McCann's record to 5-3 in the UFC, but crucially it meant back-to-back wins in the octagon.

But McCann will now face the experienced battler Goldy - who loves to share naked snaps on OnlyFans. Like many MMA fighters in recent years, Goldy has turned to the platform to share exclusive content with her fans.

Goldy has an impressive MMA record of 6-2, and she won her most recent bout against Emily Whitmire last September.

Whitmire tapped out after Goldy locked in an armbar at UFC Fight Night: Smith vs Spann. That submission victory was Goldy's first win in the UFC after landing on the promotion's radar following a win on Dana White’s Contender Series in 2019.

Goldy had been scheduled to face Jinh Yu Frey at UFC Vegas 49, but she was forced to pull out with an illness.
