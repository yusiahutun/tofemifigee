Greenville, SC: A Quick Guide to Shopping for Locals and Visitor

February 2024 · 6 minute read

Greenville, South Carolina is a city known for its Southern charm and progressive spirit. It has a thriving fashion scene with a mix of traditional and contemporary styles. Walking down the streets, one can’t help but notice the sheer range of fashion that can be spotted. You see elegant southern belle dresses that almost take you back to an earlier time and modern streetwear that reminds you that this is 2023. Greenville’s fashion scene is truly surprising, and it’s no wonder why.

The city is home to several unique boutiques and stores with their own distinct styles and offerings, as well as big shopping centers that feature the best international brands. In this article, we’ll be taking a closer look at some of the most popular shopping destinations in Greenville and what you can expect to find in this lovely city.

Table of Contents

Agusta Road

Augusta Road is a shopping district in Greenville known for its local charm and laid-back atmosphere. This historic area is lined with charming brick buildings and tree-lined sidewalks making it a picturesque destination for shopping and dining. More importantly, Augusta Road is one of the best areas to find many a women’s clothing store and boutique in Greenville, SC. These quaint places offer everything from casual jeans to the finest evening dresses and accessories. Stores here sell everything, from shoes and jewelry to all sorts of fun gifts and souvenirs. 

Augusta road is known for its locally owned business that’s chock full of unique items that are rare to find elsewhere. These range from handcrafted pottery to vintage furniture and more. Gifts from such stores have a charm you can’t find elsewhere. It’s the ultimate place to peruse if you are looking for something out of the ordinary. 

In addition to its shopping offerings, Augusta Road also has a strong sense of community. It’s a popular destination for locals and visitors alike, and it isn’t uncommon to see families strolling the sidewalks or grabbing a bite to eat at one of the area’s many cafes and restaurants. Augusta Road offers a unique shopping experience where you can spend time in a charming and relaxed environment making it definitely worth a visit.

Haywood Mall

Haywood Mall is one of the largest malls in the state and is packed with over 100 stores, such as Belk, Dillard’s, Macy’s, and JCPenney. The mall is a favorite hang-out spot for locals and is a hub for social and cultural activities. 

Throughout the year, Haywood Mall hosts various events, such as fashion shows, live music performances, and holiday celebrations, creating a festive atmosphere that brings people together. For many residents of Greenville, Haywood Mall is more than just a shopping center – it is a central part of their community and daily lives.

Greenridge Shopping Center

This outdoor shopping mall features a variety of chain stores and locally owned businesses, offering a diverse array of shopping options for visitors. You can find everything from clothing and accessories to home goods and electronics here. Some of the stores you can find here are Lowe’s, PetSmart, Total Wine, Off Broadway Shoes, World Market, Talbots, and Ulta Beauty. Stopping by Greenridge is super convenient due to its accessible location and ample parking options. The mall is easily reached from major roads, making it a quick and convenient destination for shoppers. 

Aside from its shopping offerings, The Shops at Greenridge are also known for their relaxed and inviting atmosphere. The mall’s wide sidewalks and lush landscaping provide a pleasant environment for strolling and browsing. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of dining options, from quick bites to sit-down restaurants, ensuring that they can fuel up and keep shopping all day long.

Some Must-Visit Spots if You’re Passing By Greenville

Some of the best tourist spots in South Carolina exist in Greenville, and if you have a few days to spare, you should seriously consider stopping by these prime attractions.

Falls Park on the Reedy

Also known as the birthplace of the city, this park features sights of the Reedy River and its large waterfall, which is a favorite of kayakers. One of the most notable features of Falls Park on the Reedy is its signature suspension bridge that’s over 360 feet long and spans the Reedy River, offering a unique and unforgettable experience for visitors.

The GHS Swamp Rabbit Trail

The GHS Swamp Rabbit Trail is a 19.9-mile multi-use trail located in Greenville, South Carolina. It is a converted railroad line that has been transformed into a scenic trail for hiking, biking, and walking.

The trail is known for its beautiful landscape, which includes rolling hills, lush forests, and scenic river views. The trail is also home to a variety of wildlife, including birds, rabbits, and other small animals, making it a popular destination for wildlife observation.

Paris Mountain State Park

Paris Mountain State Park is a 1,540-acre park located near Greenville, South Carolina. It was established in 1930 and is one of the oldest parks in the state. The park features hiking trails, picnic areas, campgrounds, fishing, and boating opportunities. 

The park also has a scenic lake and a historic Civilian Conservation Corps lodge that can be rented for events. Paris Mountain State Park is a popular destination for outdoor recreation and offers a variety of activities for visitors to enjoy.

Restaurants to Try Out in Greenville

Greenville also has a lot of wonderful restaurants that you can stop at after a full day of shopping, with cuisines ranging from classic Italian-style pizzas to great steakhouses and Indian food. In fact, Chris Pratt recently stopped by Southern Culture, a local restaurant in Greenville. 

Kitchen Sync, which is on Laurens Road, offers delicious vegetarian and vegan options with options such as salads, tacos, sandwiches, and specialty pizzas for lunch.

You can also stop by Ruth’s Chris Steakhouse at the Embassy Suites Hotel, 250 Riverplace, which is quite close to the Reedy River. Their sizzling steaks and variety of handcrafted cocktails make it a perfect place for a great end to your day.

Conclusion

Greenville, South Carolina, offers a ton of wonderful shopping experiences that you can enjoy. The southern charm lingers over the streets, and the tourist spots and cuisine available to you are simply to die for.  

The number of boutiques and interesting novelty stores on Augusta road and the variety of chain stores at the different shopping centers combine to provide an experience that’s truly enjoyable for everyone.
