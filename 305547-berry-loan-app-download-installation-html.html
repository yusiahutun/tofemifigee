Have you heard of the newest mobile loan app in Kenya? Berry loan app is the latest mobile money lending application. It credits users with low-cost loans and offers flexible repayment methods. Berry loan application enables users to obtain credit services via their mobile phones. This means that the user can apply for credit services and get them instantly even while on the move.

However, with Berry, that is a thing of the past. The Berry loan application is owned by Finberry Capital Limited.

Berry loan Android app download

Below is a complete guide on how you can download the application on Google Play Store.

No coding or any technical knowledge is needed here.

  • Make sure you have not restricted background data. If you are connected to any Wi-Fi network, then start with step number 2.
  • Next, open Google Play Store.
  • Search for “Berry loan”: Use the search box available in the uppermost part of your screen. Put in your keyword and press the search button. Wait for some time for the results to be processed. Once you locate the app, click on it.
  • Then click on the “Install” button. You will receive a message prompting for installation permission.
  • Click on the “Accept” button.

    Withdrawing your Berry money

    Upon successful registration and application, Berry loan is deposited into your M-Pesa account. Therefore, once you are approved, you will get immediate access to your money. From the M-Pesa account, you can withdraw the money from an ATM or via M-Pesa agent.

    How to repay the credit

    To make repayments, you are required to follow the steps below:

  • Go to Mpesa in your Safaricom menu on your phone.
  • Choose Lipa na Mpesa.
  • Select the Paybill option.
  • Enter Berry loan app Paybill number which is 977888.
  • Enter your Mpesa number in the field of the account number. The number should be the same with the one you received your credit.
  • Enter the amount to repay.
  • Enter your Mpesa PIN.
  • Confirm that all details are correct and press okay.
