Kirsten Dunst on starting a family with fiance Jesse Plemons before directing

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Kirsten Dunst is planning to put her directorial career on hold to start a family with her Fargo co-star fiancé Jesse Plemons.

The 35-year-old actress - who has been working in Hollywood since she was three-years-old - is currently in the process of writing and directing the movie adaptation of Sylvia Plath's The Bell Jar, which was set for release in 2018. She didn't specify as to whether she had plans to delay the film's release.

But the self-described "homebody" admitted it's not the "focus of her life" at the moment, telling The Sunday Times: "When I think about the age I'm at, I probably would enjoy directing a little later from now, you know?

"But the focus in my life is... Well, I'm redoing a house. I have other things I'm enjoying and I've been working since I was three. I'm a homebody. I'm trying to make it all happen. The movie industry isn't everything to me at all.

"I love what I do, when I love the people I'm working with, but other than that it's not really worth it to me."

After 30 decades in the film industry, Dunst recently said that it was "time to have babies and chill" during an interview with Marie Claire. And she's now saying that that she'd rather expand her family before she makes her move behind the camera.

"I'd almost rather just write screenplays, as directing is so much of your life," she went on. "Also, I'm in a place now where I do want to direct, but I more want to have a family."

Meanwhile, the Fargo star also clarified her response to director Sofia Coppola asking her to lose weight for The Beguiled during a joint chat with Coppola for Variety.

"I didn't say no," Dunst stated. "I made a joke, because we shot in this area called Thibodaux, where the only options were fast food. I was, like, how can I lose weight? Left to my own devices, personally, I really can't."

Dunst and actor Plemons confirmed their engagement earlier this year, with Dunst adding to E! Online they plan to tie the knot next spring .
