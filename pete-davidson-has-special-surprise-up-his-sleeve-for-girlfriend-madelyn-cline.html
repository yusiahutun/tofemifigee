Pete Davidson has a 'special surprise' up his sleeve for girlfriend Madelyn Cline

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Pete Davidson is still going strong with girlfriend Madelyn Cline, months after dating rumors surfaced. The comedian also has a ‘special surprise’ for his beau on her 26th birthday, a source claims.

Pete Davidson’s dating history has sparked many conversations over the years. The SNL comedian has dated some of the most beautiful and successful women in the world. He is also known for bouncing back fast after a breakup. The celebrity usually has a new woman in her life within a few weeks or months after his previous relationship ends. However, things seem to getting serious between him and Outer Banks star Madelyn Cline.

Pete Davidson has a ‘special surprise’ for girlfriend Madelyn Cline

A source exclusively told US Weekly the 30-year-old comedian has some special surprise up his sleeve for his actress girlfriend. The TV star has planned a special gift for Madelyn’s birthday as well as for Christmas.

“Pete and Madelyn’s romance is still going strong. Pete has some special surprises in store for her birthday and for Christmas,” the source said. They added The King of Staten Island star “truly cares” about his new girlfriend.

“He truly cares about her and wants to show her how much she means to him,” the source said. The duo has a four-year age gap, with Pete being 30 and Madelyn turning 26 on December 21. The two struck up a romance on the heels of Pete’s breakup with City on Fire star Chase Sui Wonders.

Pete’s mom and sister are fond of Madelyn

The source also told US Weekly Pete, who is now a movie star, has changed in the eyes of his loved ones. His family apparently sees a difference in his dynamic with the Outer Banks star that they did not in his past relationships.

Both his mom and sister adore The Originals actress. The source also says Pete’s sister Casey is closer to Madelyn than she was with a few other women he dated.

“Pete’s mom and sister completely support this relationship. His sister, Casey, adores Madelyn and is closer to her than she’s been to some of the other exes he’s dated in the past,” the source said.

The SNL star’s family appreciates Madelyn does not feel the need to put their relationship on “full display” for the world. “It makes them feel like the feelings they have for each other are genuine, which they respect,” the source further said.

This comment would make many fans think of Pete’s relationship with Kim Kardashian. Their romance was notoriously public from the get-go. Kim often posted pictures of kissing her then-beau. The two also appeared at the 2022 Met Gala together.

The news of Pete and Chase Sui Wonders’ split hit the news in August 2023. Just a month later, Pete sparked dating rumors with Madelyn Cline.

Pete and Chase publically dated for about eight months, from December 2022. In the summer, a source told Entertainment Tonight the pair had split up. The next month, the star was linked to Netflix star Madelyn.

“Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders have broken up and both of them are focusing on themselves,” the statement read. Now, another source is telling US Weekly there is “no bad blood or any hard feelings” between the exes. The Bodies Bodies Bodies co-stars’ “relationship had simply run its course.”
