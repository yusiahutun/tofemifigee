The History of Estádio Monumental de Núñez

Estádio Monumental de Núñez, also known as the Monumental, is a renowned football stadium located in Buenos Aires, Argentina. It is the home stadium of Club Atlético River Plate, one of the most successful football clubs in South America. The stadium was inaugurated on May 25, 1938, and has since become an iconic landmark in the world of football.

The Largest Stadium in Argentina

The Estádio Monumental de Núñez holds the distinction of being the largest stadium in Argentina, with a seating capacity of over 70,000 spectators. Its sheer size and grandeur make it a sight to behold, attracting football fans from all over the world.
