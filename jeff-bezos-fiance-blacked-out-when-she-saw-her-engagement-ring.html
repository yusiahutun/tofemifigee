Jeff Bezos' fianc blacked out when she saw her engagement ring

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Lauren Sánchez has revealed she almost ‘blacked’ out when she saw the size of her engagement ring from fiancé Jeff Bezos, and honestly, we don’t blame her.

The couple got engaged on a luxurious yacht trip in May, where the Amazon chairman surprised his partner with a huge pink diamond.

Jeff Bezos’ fiance Lauren Sánchez ‘blacked out’ when she saw her engagement ring

As if a yacht trip wasn’t good enough, Lauren was surprised with a huge diamond ring from her fiancé, and even she couldn’t believe it.

“When he opened the box, I think I blacked out a bit,” Sánchez said in Vogue’s December issue.

To add to the surprise, Bezos hid the ring under her pillow, which she discovered before going to bed.

The exact price of the ring is unknown, however, experts tell Page Six that it appears to clock in at 20 carats and cost roughly $2.5 million.

As for the wedding, the 53-year-old tells the publication that they’re still thinking about what they’re going to do for their big day.

“We’re still thinking about the wedding, what it’s going to be. Is it going to be big? Is it going to be overseas? We don’t know yet. We’ve only been engaged five months.”

However, there is one thing she knows for sure, that she’ll definitely be taking her husband’s last name.

“Will she be taking his name? She looks at me like I am insane. “Uh, yes, one hundred percent. I am looking forward to being Mrs. Bezos,” she says.

The couple began dating in 2019, shortly after Bezos got divorced from his first wife, MacKenzie Scott. Lauren also divorced her first husband, Patrick Whitesell in the same year.

Inside the couple’s A-List engagement party

As their Vogue pics come out, the couple is reported to have celebrated their engagement this weekend, with a guest list of A-List celebrities.

The party is said to have taken place in Beverly Hills and was attended by Kim and Khloe Kardashian, Mick Jagger, Oprah Winfrey, and Robert Pattinson, to name a few.

Nineties singer Jewel is also said to have performed at the extravaganza, with guests partying in a throwback disco room.

If the party is this big, we can’t wait to see the wedding!
