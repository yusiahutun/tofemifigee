Julia Roberts and George Clooney Are the Coolest Duo in Coordinating Suits

April 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Name a more iconic friend duo than Julia Roberts and George Clooney.

Yesterday, the actors stepped out together for a joint appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live! and coordinated ultra-cool suit looks.

Roberts looked incredible in a beige-and-black number from the Gucci x Adidas collaboration. It included a double-breasted beige suit jacket with golden buttons, black faux fur at the sleeves, Adidas's signature black stripes running down the sides, and the Gucci x Adidas logo on the chest. The beige, wide-leg pleated pants had the same stripes down the sides and were folded at the bottom; Roberts wore them with a pair of shiny black pointed-toe pumps.

Under the suit, she wore a crisp white dress shirt and black tie, and made the ensemble even cooler with some black aviator shades, golden jewelry, and a black manicure. Her hair was down in loose waves, and she wore a natural makeup look.

RB/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

She walked arm in arm with Clooney, who looked dapper as always. The actor wore a gray suit, black dress shirt, and black dress shoes, accessorizing only with blue-tinted sunglasses, a silver watch, and his silver wedding band.

RB/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Clooney and Roberts star together in the romantic comedy Ticket to Paradise. In recent weeks, while promoting the movie, the actors have spoken of their experience filming together for so many months in Australia. Roberts told The New York Times that longtime friend Clooney saved her from "complete loneliness and despair" during the time away from her family. And in a TODAY show interview, Clooney joked about how awkward it was to kiss his best friend for the film.

RB/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Ticket to Paradise premieres October 21 and follows a divorced couple who travel to Bali to try to stop their daughter's wedding.
