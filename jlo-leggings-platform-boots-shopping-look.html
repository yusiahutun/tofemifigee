Jennifer Lopez Wore Leather Leggings &amp; Platform DSW Boots in NYC

June 2024 · 2 minute read

J.Lo kept it simple and stylish while out in New York City.

Yesterday, the superstar was photographed taking care of presumably some holiday shopping and wearing a sleek but comfortable look while doing so. Dressed in a pair of skintight leather leggings and a matching black turtleneck, she sported platform combat boots from her own JLo x DSW footwear line. She completed her ensemble with a chic camel-hued overcoat by Max Mara, oversized tinted sunglasses, and a protective face mask. As for beauty, the singer went with a neat slicked-back bun and a winter-appropriate deep shade of burgundy polish.

Gotham//Getty Images

Taking to social media earlier that day, J.Lo shared with fans that her latest venture, JLo Beauty, sold out in a single day during its soft launch. The skincare line, inspired by the beauty secrets Lopez inherited from her mother, comprises face masks, a super-hydrating serum, luxe creams, an everyday cleanser, and more.

"I just heard that we sold out of our first drop of JLo Beauty," said Lopez during an impromptu recording session from her private car. "I just had to get on and say thank you to you guys. We have been working so hard on this, and I'm so excited that you are as excited as I am about it! Don't worry if you didn't get it today, because it's available on January 1 for everybody at midnight, at New Year's—new year, new skin, new you! I cannot wait for everybody to try it. But thank you, guys, for your response right now."
