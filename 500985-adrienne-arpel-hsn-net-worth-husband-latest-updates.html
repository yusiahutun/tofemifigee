Adrienne Arpel's biography

Adrien Joachim was born on 15th July 1941 in New Jersey City. Her mother (Ada Stark) was of Polish heritage, born in Montreal, Canada, while her father (Samuel Joachin) was of Russian heritage, born in the United States.

Does Adrienne Arpel have any siblings? Yes, she has an elder sister, Marilyn Joachin. After her primary school education, she joined high school, graduating in 1959. Later she went to Pace University between 1960 and 1962.

Career

Adrienne launched her business in 1959 when she was only 18. The star launched the business with $400 she had earned from babysitting. After her high school graduation, she opened her first shop in Englewood, NJ.

Later, she ventured into business after receiving conflicting advice from staff selling beauty products in department stores. Therefore, she saw the need to educate customers on skin care and the best products to use as per their ingredients.
