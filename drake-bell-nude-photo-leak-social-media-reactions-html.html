Megan from Drake and Josh now

Drake Bell music career

Since appearing on Drake and Josh, Drake Bell has done a lot of voice acting work, but for the most part he's been focused on his music career. 

It all started with Found A Way, a song written and performed by Bell. The song was featured as the Drake and Josh theme song. 

In 2006, Bell signed with Universal Motown. He released his first single, I Know, in October of that year. His album, It's Only Time, came out in December 2006 and charted at No. 81 on the Billboard 200.

Bell released another single, called Terrific, in 2011. The song was one of a handful included on his EP, A Reminder. He followed that up with the release of a holiday single, called Christmas Promise. 

In 2014, Bell released Bitchcraft, the first single off his album Ready Steady Go! The album was the first he released with Surfdog Records.

Bell released another EP, called Honest, in 2017. 

He put out Rewind in February 2018, but hasn't yet released a full album. His music is available on Spotify, YouTube and other such services.
