Model Jasmine Tookes' Beauty &amp; Fashion Tips

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Media Platforms Design Team

Best beauty tip you've learned from Victoria's Secret?

Victoria's Secret always likes when girls have a good glow and highlight, so I always use a highlighter stick and good moisturizer. I like Nars highlighting sticks and baby oil.

Workout regimen?

I work out at Equinox five times a week. A lot of weight-training and squats—tons of that.

Favorite NYC restaurant?

I love Buddakan—it's so beautiful inside.

Halloween costume?

I'm thinking about being Princess Jasmine. My name's Jasmine and I've never done it, so maybe this year.

Go-to daily uniform?

Black jeans and a white t-shirt.

What has your stylist mom taught you about fashion?

She's a celebrity stylist and growing up, I was always on set with her, so that's kind of how I started modeling. I've definitely learned to pay attention when I'm shopping because so many things can have rips or tears that you don't notice until you get home. She always examines every little detail.

Favorite thing to do when you're off-duty?

I'm always shopping, I have an addiction. I love Victoria's Secret, of course, and Club Monaco and Barney's.

What are you buying for fall?

A lot of neutral colors and pastels—light blues, pinks, grays, whites.

Alexandra Tunell is the Senior Digital Beauty Editor at Harper's BAZAAR, where she writes beauty features and covers industry news, health, fitness and wellness trends. She began her career in the Lucky beauty closet, then went on to work at Allure. When she's not testing the latest skin care treatments, the Los Angeles native is searching the city for the best Mexican food, binge-watching Bravo and escaping to the beach as often as possible.
