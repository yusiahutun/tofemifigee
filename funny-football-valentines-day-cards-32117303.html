Five funniest football Valentine's cards you can still buy at the last minute

October 2024 · 4 minute read

For Spurs fans, new manager Ange Postecoglou is the one they're loving this Valentine's.

But if your eyes are drawn elsewhere, and you're in need of card-based inspiration, look no further. England stars, all-time greats, and some of football's most eccentric and beloved characters feature on the front of some of these quite brilliant gifts.

No one is safe when it comes to hilarious Valentine's spoof-like cards, it seems. And here at Daily Star Sport, we've got you covered for some last-minute card options to get your other half.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Initially for Real Madrid, and since for the likes of Manchester United and Al Nassr, Cristiano Ronaldo's celebration has to be one of the most recognisable in all of football – perhaps even in all of sport.

It's been over 10 years now since Ronaldo first graced the world with his 'SIUUU' celebration. So what better way to remind the one you love, just how much you love them by channeling your inner CR7.

The Aston Villa gaffer coined his famous 'good ebining' phrase whilst at Arsenal. The Spaniard would so politely address those attending his press conference, and all that happened was he got brutally mocked for it.

Now, taking Villa to the lofty heights of the top four is his goal. And this card is perfect for anyone wishing to pay homage to the great man, who could yet deliver European glory at Villa Park in the form of the Europa Conference League trophy, 12 months after David Moyes achieved the same feat with West Ham.

Ole Gunnar Solskjær

Sainsbury's Christmas advert star Rick Astley and his infamous 'Never Gonna Give You Up' inspires this card based on former United goalscorer and gaffer Solskjær.

Though it might not bring similar joy to a last gasp Champions League final-winning strike against Bayern Munich, it could still be appreciated if you're running low on ideas. Just like how Sir Alex Ferguson used to turn to the super-sub when he was out of options in the dugout.

Jack Grealish

The England and Manchester City ace has a set of world-renowned calves. He insists he never really works on them, though, and they're purely down to genetics!

Fans of treble-winning 'Super Jack', City or the England team should see this card as the perfect gift if they think their partner is the best thing since sliced bread.
