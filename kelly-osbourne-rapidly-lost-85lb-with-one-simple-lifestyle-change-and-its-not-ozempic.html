Kelly Osbourne 'rapidly lost' 85lb with one simple lifestyle change - and it's not Ozempic

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Kelly Osbourne has been showing off her amazing figure, after losing 85lb following the birth of her son, and she’s now put a stop to the rumors she’d used weight loss drug Ozempic.

The 39-year-old and her boyfriend Sid Wilson welcomed their baby boy Sidney, at the end of 2022. Since then, Kelly Osbourne has lost 85lb, and she’s now revealing how she did it with on simple lifestyle change.

Kelly Osbourne reveals how she lost 85lb following birth of son

“I know everybody thinks I took Ozempic,” the former reality TV star told Extra. “I did not take Ozempic.  I don’t know where that came from. My mom (Sharon Osbourne) took Ozempic.”

So how did she do it? “I had gestational diabetes and I had to lose the weight that I had gained during pregnancy,” Kelly told the outlet. “Otherwise I was at a higher risk of actually getting diabetes, which I did not want.”

She then explained that she “cut out sugar and carbohydrates” and “rapidly lost weight.”

The star has previously used other ways to help her lose weight. Around five years ago, the mom of one underwent gastric sleeve surgery.

Her mother, Sharon Osbourne has been extremely open about her experience using Ozempic, and the side effects that came with it.

“For me, the first few weeks was f***ing s*** because you just throw up all the time, and feel so nauseous,” she previously told The Club Random with Bill Maher podcast.

However, she said the nausea wore off after a couple of weeks, and ‘felt fine’ afterwards.

The star debuts another transformation

As well as her weight loss transformation, Kelly has now undergone a hair transformation, ditching her signature purple locks.

After spending six years with the dramatic look, it seems like the Fashion Police star thought it was time for a change as she debuted a chic pastel pink look on Instagram.

“Did you hear I have a new look?” she captioned the post. Fans were loving the change as one commented: “Absolutely stunning!”

“CHEFS KISS!” exclaimed another.
