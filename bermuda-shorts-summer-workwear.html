your answer to summer chic

March 2024 · 3 minute read

In warmer weather, it can become more difficult to decide on what will not only keep you cool, but what will also look smart and sophisticated - particularly in a professional environment (if you are, in fact, going to the office right now). If you have been struggling with this particular wardrobe dilemma this summer, then let us convince you to embrace the Bermuda short.

The style was everywhere on the spring/summer 2019 catwalks, from bright red at Emilia Wickstead to printed at Prada – and, a year on, it's clear that the short style is here to stay with plenty of stylish women adopting the trend into their SS20 wardrobes, too.

The Bermuda short became a common go-to on the streets of Copenhagen Fashion Week this season, as well as featuring in the collections of some of the world's most influential brands, that of Ganni and Jacquemus for spring/summer 2021.

Getty Images

L-R Copenhagen Fashion Week street style, Jacquemus spring/summer 2021, CPFW street style, Ganni spring/summer 2021

Think of it as the cycling short’s more sophisticated older sister, with the same knee-grazing length but a more grown-up, less skin-hugging feel. The slim-leg fit and tailored style allows for an on-trend and undeniably cool take on office-wear for the summer months.

Convinced to give the style a go? Below, we present four ways to wear them.

Denim

Denim puts a Nineties spin on the Bermuda short and gives the style a more casual, wearable feel. Dress up a denim pair by styling with sleek mules, a statement bag and a crisp white shirt worn untucked.
