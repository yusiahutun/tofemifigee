Arizona Del Taco manager lays into customers with profanity-laden rant

April 2024 Â· 3 minute read

They only wanted the sauces they had ordered - instead two customers at a Peoria, Arizona drive-thru got a foul-mouthed rant from the manager, who boasted that he couldn't be fired because his dad was the owner.

And when they pointed out that they were recording the Del Taco manager's appalling behavior, he claimed that nobody would watch it.

But he was very wrong: in the three days since it was uploaded to Vimeo, the video has been watched 413,000 times - and he has been fired.

Insult: The video begins with the Del Taco manager (left, in the background) flipping the bird to a pair of customers who complained they didn't get the sauces they ordered. When he starts talking he is unrepentant

The video begins after the unnamed couple have returned to the drive-thru to pick up sauces that they had ordered but not been given, as well as extra tacos.

'This guy right here, he just called me a "f****** dick,"' the driver complains, as the manager flips him off in the background.

'Did he give you the food?' the woman who is filming the video asks.

'No,' he shrugs. 'But I didn't pay for it either.'

The manager returns with a cocky grin, and when the woman complains about his attitude he shrugs and points at the camera. 'I don't give a f***, bro,' he says.

'Shoot it, go ahead... Where you gonna post it? YouTube? No-one's gonna f***ing see it.'

Some 413,000 viewers would disagree, but the man, unaware of what's coming, keeps digging his hole.

When the woman says she's going to send the video to his boss, he replies, 'I am the boss! My dad's actually the f***ing owner, so good luck with that.'

'I don't give a f***, bro': The manager says he doesn't care that they're filming him - he says nobody will watch the video. In three days it racked up 413,000 views - with Del Taco management among the viewers

He goes on to say that he's 'done this a billion times' without being closed down. 

'You guys give me s*** all the time,' he continued. 'I'm not talking about you specifically, I'm talking about you the customers.'

When they ask him why he doesn't just quit, he smugly responds: 'Bro, why would I quit? This is my f***ing fortune, right here! Do you know how much of a pot of gold this is?'

The couple eventually drive off, with the manager calling them 'dumba** b*****s' as they go.

But the manager's pot of gold is now empty thanks to the video, which eventually made it back to Del Taco headquarters.

'We strive to provide a positive guest experience at all of our restaurants and were appalled by this behavior as it is not representative of our high guest service standards,' a Del Taco spokesperson told ABC 15 Friday.

'We took this incident very seriously and addressed it with the location's franchisee, who took definitive action by terminating the employee immediately.'

Seems that being the boss's son doesn't guarantee you a job after all.
