5 royal teddy bears to gift your little Prince or Princess

March 2024 · 4 minute read

It’s fair to say that a teddy bear is about as classic a gift for a newborn or child as they come, hitting the marks as something thoughtful, and a keepsake.

And in a world where interactive toys, kid-friendly tablets and learning screens are par for the course, the good old teddy bear still reigns as a solid, well-received gift that a child will love for years to come. Since we’re royal-fans, we thought one of the cutest gifts you could give a little prince or princess, or rather King and Queen, would be a royal teddy bear, a cuddly little thing with a crown or royal-inspired design, be it a Beefeater, a ted with a tiara or one that is giving all the regal feels.

How popular were royal teddy bears during the coronation?

With all the royal memorabilia available throughout the coronation, it was a coronation teddy bear that reigned as a popular keepsake.

Dayna Isom Johnson, Etsy Trend Expert commented: "With the majority of the nation never having witnessed a royal coronation before many chose to mark the occasion with a piece of memorabilia and royal teddy bears were one of the top products of choice. In March this year Etsy saw a 2652% increase in searches for King Coronation items showing just how much the whole country got swept up in Royal fever!"

Whether it’s for a newborn or `birthday gift, the HELLO! shopping team have found the best royal teddy bears to shop. How we chose the best royal teddy bears

  • Price: We’ve considered a range of toys with differing price points. Prices range from £10 for a personalised bear to £150 and upwards for a designer branded teddy.
  • Quality: We’ve searched for the teddy bears that have received the best reviews from shoppers. Although we’ve not been able to personally touch each toy, we’ve handpicked the bears that receive, overall, the most positive reviews.
  • Appearance: No-one wants a tired looking teddy, and we’ve chosen the ones we consider to be the cutest.
  • Size: From mini teddy bears to extra-large stuffed toys, we’ve brought you a range of sizes to fit each type of recipient, from babies to older kids and adults too.
