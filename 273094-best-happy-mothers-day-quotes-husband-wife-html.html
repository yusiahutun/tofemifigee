When is mother’s day?

It is majorly celebrated in the month of May and in some parts of the world, in March. It is usually put on the second Sunday of May since many people are more available during the weekend than weekdays. This years mothers day in Kenya is set to be on the 12th of May, 2019 which has already people buzzing about it, already giving them a nostalgic feeling as per what happened last year. I would definitely say that mother’s day brings people together and brings an awesomeness to mothers even when they tend to think they don’t deserve to be awesome.
