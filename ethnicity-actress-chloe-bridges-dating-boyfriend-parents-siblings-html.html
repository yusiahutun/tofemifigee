Chloe Bridges And Adam Devine: Dating? Relationship?

Chloe Bridges and Adam DeVine have been dating since 2015, and by looking at them, the couple has a pretty strong and happy relationship.

According to US Magazine, Chloe and her boyfriend first met each other when they started working in the 2015 horror movie, The Final Girls. An insider spilled,

"They're having a lot of fun."

As of 2019, the couple is still together and were seen in a group shot from New Year’s Eve on Instagram joined by Zac Efron and his girlfriend, Sami Miro. Chloe also celebrated her 24th birthday with her beau on 27 December 2015 in Chicago.
