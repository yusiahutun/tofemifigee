Tamra Judge And Teddi Mellencamp Completely Rip Teresa Giudice's Wedding Look To Shreds

June 2024 Â· 2 minute read

"Real Housewives of Orange County" star Tamra Judge and former "Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" star Teddi Mellencamp have made it clear that they are not fans of Teresa Giudice's wedding look. During an episode of their podcast, "Two T's In A Pod," the reality stars gave their unfiltered thoughts on Teresa's over-the-top hair for the event. They even compared it to the way she looked at her first wedding to ex-husband Joe Giudice.

Judge said, "I mean I hate to rip on anybody on their wedding day ... but why didn't not one person say to her 'No! God, no!'" Mellencamp touched on the fact that the look reportedly cost Teresa about $10,000 and assumed she listed the price in case someone wanted to redo the look for their big day. Judge, however, did not agree and replied, "Okay, no. Nobody's going to. And like I said, if I were her, I would be in hiding." It's clear that these housewives are not friends with Giudice, as they confirmed they were not invited to the wedding.

However, several other stars from the "Real Housewives" franchise were invited and attended the big day. According to Page Six, Cynthia Bailey, Dorinda Medley, Phaedra Parks, and several other reality stars were there to celebrate. Giudice is not one to bite her tongue, so it's only a matter of time before she responds to Judge and Mellencamp's rude remarks.
