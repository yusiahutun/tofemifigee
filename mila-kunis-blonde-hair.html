Mila Kunis Just Debuted Bleach Blonde Hair

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Mila Kunis has rarely strayed from her signature shade of glossy brown hair over the years, but the actress just stepped out wearing a dramatic new look. Kunis, who is married to Ashton Kutcher, was seen out in Los Angeles with long bleach blonde hair dip-dyed with turquoise tips. This wasn't just a hair color change—it's a major transformation.

The new hair color is likely for an upcoming role. Deadline reports that Kunis signed on to the film Four Good Days opposite Glenn Close: "Deb (Close), a mother helping her daughter Molly (Kunis) work through four crucial days on Molly’s road to recovery from substance abuse. Their time together after a long estrangement revives both the painful wounds they have inflicted on each other, but also offers the opportunity to rekindle their fundamental, complicated, love for one another."

Kunis has worn various different haircuts over the years—from short lobs to wispy bangs and ultra-glossy sleek hair. But the actress isn't a beauty junkie when she's not on the red carpet or starring in films. "I don’t wear makeup. I don’t wash my hair every day," she told Glamour. "It’s not something that I associate with myself. I commend women who wake up 30, 40 minutes early to put on eyeliner. I think it’s ­beautiful. I’m just not that person."

For now, Kunis is keeping her new hair color under wraps. She was spotted by paparazzi wearing a baseball cap to disguise the new shade of blonde and teal.
