A new breast firming gel dubbed Boob Glue promises to give women all day 'ooh la la lift, hold and support'.

The product, which is set to launch next month, was the brainchild of California-based beautician Dawn Jackson.

After spending years experimenting with push-up bras, inserts, and even electrical tape she set about creating a 'gravity defying adhesive' to eliminate 'sagging', 'bulging' and the dreaded 'quadra boob'.

'It has been my genuine passion... to bring to life a product that does what it promises and makes a woman feel and look amazing.'

Product innovation: Boob Glue is set to hit the shelves next month

Directions on the glue stick instruct users to roll the adhesive over the chest area, and secure each breast to a 'favorite bra' in the desired position.

It is recommended for women with natural and augmented breasts and can be used with a mastectomy bra or breast prosthesis.

Ms Jackson refers to the product as a 'boob job in a bottle' and 'your girl’s breast friend.'

Before and after shots on her professional Facebook page show how the product provides instant lift.

'Nice results! Gave her the cleavage she never thought possible,' one caption reads.

And another explains: 'Wearing her fav VS push up Bra, we applied the product and ....the after pic is OOO-LA-LA! Same bra, no padding added, just the product.'

Before turning her attention to Boob Glue Ms Jackson worked as a make-up artist, hairstylist and image consultant.

Similar products on the market include Rodial's Boob Job Gel, which claims to boost a woman's bust size by half a cup. However it is only available in the UK.

Currently there is no word where Boob Glue will be stocked or how much it will retail for.
