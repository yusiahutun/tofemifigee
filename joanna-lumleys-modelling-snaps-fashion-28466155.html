Joanna Lumley's modelling snaps - fashion icon, tiny crop top and Amanda Holden vibes

July 2024 · 5 minute read

TV legend Joanna Lumley became an icon with BBC viewers when she played Patsy in hit comedy Absolutely Fabulous.

Her and Jennifer Saunders' Edwina not only acted fabulous, but they looked it too, with the pair rocking a number of beautiful looks throughout the series.

Joanna was no stranger to style however, as the famous star also wowed fans with a series of seriously stunning modelling snaps during the sixties.

Joanna Lumley began her modelling career in the sixties and it seems that the star has not aged a day.

In a gorgeous black and white snap from 1970, the star flaunted her gorgeous curves as she lay on a sofa in a patterned dress.

With Joanna's brunette locks full of typical sixties volume and flowing past her shoulders, it is no surprise that the star became an instant pin-up in this seriously glam snap.

Sixties chic

Joanna Lumley has been the very essence of high fashion for more than five decades, and it is very easy to see why.

In 1968, the star looked effortlessly cool as she posed on top of a red Ferrari in a long white, high-collared coat, paired with red shoes that matched the car beside her.

Joanna looked unrecognisable with dark brunette locks in the snap, but every inch as fabulous as ever, with her smoky eyes and pinned up hair adding an extra touch of chic to the look.

Needless to say, it seems that Joanna is one of the original fashion influencers – and we still cannot get enough of her looks more than 50 years on.

Racy snap

Joanna looked seductive as she posed in a tiny top made of silver coil circles in a seriously saucy modelling snap from 1970.

The star lay on a sofa as she looked into the distance while wearing the silver crop top in an eye-popping snap that flaunted her toned abs.

Showcasing her slender figure, the look was finished with lashings of mascara that framed Joanna's beautiful eyes, a dash of clear, plumping lip gloss and red, voluminous waves to her shoulders in the sexy snap.

Beach beauty

In 1967, Joanna soaked up the sun for a stunning beachfront snap.

The star flaunted her endless legs in a gorgeous blue and red polo shirt inspired bathing suit, as she showed off her sun kissed skin and honey coloured locks alongside a fellow model.

Joanna looked radiant as she let her natural beauty shine through in a barely there make-up look in the sizzling snap, with her trademark fringe also framing her gorgeous face.

Effortlessly cool

In the mid-seventies, Joanna showcased a chic, everyday look as she posed in matching a denim waistcoat and jeans.

Looking fabulous in the tiny waistcoat and high-waisted jeans, the star lifted her toned arms into a stunning pose as she gazed towards the camera.

Wearing a barely there make-up look and once again rocking brunette waves, the outfit was a sign of the effortlessly glamorous looks that we have come to know and love from the inspirational star.

Amanda Holden vibes

Joanna posed for another beautiful beach photograph in 1967, when she was snapped on a beach front in a gorgeous pink and orange stripe dress.

Paired with pink tights and red heels, Joanna looked like a multi-coloured fantasy, with the look completed by a pink hair scarf and floral bracelet for an extra edgy twist.

That's not all, as Joanna looks a dead-ringer for Britain's Got Talent judge Amanda Holden in this throwback snap – and we could certainly see Amanda rocking this look too.

Avengers pin-up

As the seventies drew in, Joanna became one of the stars of TV series The New Avengers, where she played Purdey between 1976 to 1977.

In the show she rocked a number of seriously sexy looks, with her trademark blonde bob becoming a feature of her outfits and modelling snaps of the time too.

One such snap saw Joanna looking sophisticated as she posed in a sexy black dress, complete with cheeky thigh high splits for an extra saucy twist.
