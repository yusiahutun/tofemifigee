Ariana Grande says nude photos are fake after being nominated for six EMAs

September 2024 · 3 minute read

She was one of dozens of female celebrities to fall victim to a nude photo leak by iCloud hackers in early September.

But Ariana Grande is adamant the images are not of her and took a moment to personally address the matter on Monday as she arrived at LAX airport.

In a TMZ video, the 21-year-old said: 'Those aren't me. It was funny though.'

Explaining why the risque pics are most definitely fake she continued: 'That's not my room, that's not my cat, that's not my belly button ring, that's not my hair, that's not my profile.

'I was like, that's not me.' she said emphatically. 

Shortly after the images appeared online, the Problem singer took to Twitter to declare that her derriere is much 'cuter' than those pictured.

'But forreal tho whoever thought those were actually me...... love u but I'm praying for u'.
